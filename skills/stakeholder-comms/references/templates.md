# Stakeholder Communication Templates

## 1. Go-Live Announcement

```markdown
Subject: [Product Name] is now live — what you need to know

## TL;DR
{Product} is now live as of {date}. {One sentence on what it does for the audience.}

## What's Live
- {Feature/capability 1}
- {Feature/capability 2}
- {Feature/capability 3}

## Who It Affects
| Group | What Changes for You |
|-------|---------------------|
| {Group 1} | {Impact description} |
| {Group 2} | {Impact description} |

## What You Need to Do
**{Group 1}:** {Action required, with deadline if applicable}
**{Group 2}:** {Action required}
**Everyone:** {General action — e.g., "bookmark the new URL"}

## Known Limitations
- {Limitation 1 — with timeline for resolution if known}
- {Limitation 2}

## Support
- **Issues:** {Channel — e.g., Slack #channel, ServiceNow, email}
- **Questions:** {Contact name and email}
- **Documentation:** {Link}

## What's Next
- {Next phase/milestone with approximate date}
```

## 2. Status Update

```markdown
Subject: {Product} Status Update — {Date Range}

## RAG Status: {GREEN / AMBER / RED}
{One sentence explaining the status.}

## Key Accomplishments
- {Accomplishment 1}
- {Accomplishment 2}
- {Accomplishment 3}

## Decisions Made
| Decision | Date | Made By | Impact |
|----------|------|---------|--------|
| {Decision} | {Date} | {Name} | {Impact} |

## Risks & Blockers
| Risk/Blocker | Severity | Owner | Mitigation | Status |
|-------------|----------|-------|------------|--------|
| {Issue} | {H/M/L} | {Name} | {Action} | {Open/Mitigated} |

## Upcoming Milestones (Next 2 Weeks)
| Milestone | Target Date | Owner | Status |
|-----------|------------|-------|--------|
| {Milestone} | {Date} | {Name} | {On Track / At Risk / Delayed} |

## Action Items
| # | Action | Owner | Due |
|---|--------|-------|-----|
| 1 | {Action} | {Name} | {Date} |

## Help Needed
- {Request 1 — who needs to do what}
```

## 3. Onboarding Brief

```markdown
Subject: Welcome to {Product} — Your Onboarding Brief

## Welcome
{Name}, welcome to the {Product} team. This brief gives you everything you need to get started.

## Product Overview
{2-paragraph summary: what the product is, why Coco Inc uses it, current state.}

## Your Role
- **Title:** {Role}
- **Scope:** {What you're responsible for}
- **Reports to:** {Manager}
- **Key relationship:** {Who you'll work with most}

## Key Contacts
| Name | Role | Email | When to Contact |
|------|------|-------|-----------------|
| {Name} | {Role} | {Email} | {Context — e.g., "for technical questions"} |

## Access Setup
- [ ] Request {Product} access via {method}
- [ ] Complete SSO enrollment
- [ ] Join Slack/Teams channels: {list}
- [ ] Bookmark: {key URLs}
- [ ] Review: {key documents with links}

## Key Documents
| Document | Purpose | Link |
|----------|---------|------|
| PRD | Requirements and scope | {path} |
| Presentation | Product overview deck | {path} |
| Meeting Notes | Recent decisions | {path} |

## First-Week Actions
1. {Day 1: Read X, meet Y}
2. {Day 2-3: Complete training Z}
3. {Day 4-5: Shadow process A, attend meeting B}

## Glossary
| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| {Term} | {Definition} |
```

## 4. Change Notification

```markdown
Subject: Planned Change — {Product}: {Summary}

## What's Changing
{Clear description of the change.}

## When
- **Change window:** {Date, time, timezone}
- **Expected duration:** {Duration}
- **Downtime:** {Yes/No — if yes, duration}

## Why
{Business or technical rationale — 2-3 sentences.}

## Who's Affected
- {Group 1}: {How they're affected}
- {Group 2}: {How they're affected}

## What You Need to Do
**Before the change:** {Any preparation needed}
**During the change:** {What to expect}
**After the change:** {Any action required — e.g., clear cache, re-login}

## Rollback Plan
If the change causes issues, we will {rollback action} within {timeframe}.

## Questions?
Contact {Name} at {email}.
```

## 5. Incident Summary

```markdown
Subject: {Product} Incident Summary — {Date}: {Brief Description}

## Severity: {SEV1-4}
**Duration:** {Start time} — {End time} ({total duration})
**Impact:** {Who/what was affected}

## Timeline
| Time | Event |
|------|-------|
| {HH:MM} | {Incident detected / reported} |
| {HH:MM} | {Response initiated} |
| {HH:MM} | {Key action taken} |
| {HH:MM} | {Resolved / service restored} |

## Root Cause
{Clear, factual explanation. No blame.}

## Resolution
{What was done to fix it.}

## Prevention
| # | Action | Owner | Target Date |
|---|--------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | {Preventive measure} | {Name} | {Date} |

## Lessons Learned
- {What went well}
- {What could improve}
```

```markdown
|---|----------|---------|---------------|---------|

## Budget & Timeline
| Item | Plan | Actual | Variance | Notes |
|------|------|--------|----------|-------|
| Budget | {$X} | {$Y} | {+/-$Z} | {explanation} |
| Timeline | {Date} | {Date} | {+/-N weeks} | {explanation} |

## Top Risks
| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation | Status |
|------|--------|------------|------------|--------|
| {Risk 1} | {H/M/L} | {H/M/L} | {Action} | {Mitigating / Monitoring / Escalating} |

## Accomplishments Since Last SteerCo

- {Accomplishment 2}

## Next Period Focus
- {Priority 1}
- {Priority 2}
```
