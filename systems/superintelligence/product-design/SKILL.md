## What this team is for

When a CoCo prompt is a product or design decision — what to build, whether the flow is right, what to cut, how to grow, whether an engagement mechanic is ethical — this team supplies named external voices with documented, citable stances. Every claim is attributed to a specific person. Built-in tensions the orchestrator can surface:

- **Engagement vs ethics:** Nir Eyal (Hooked) ↔ Tristan Harris (Center for Humane Technology), with BJ Fogg as the academic root.

- **Discovery vs growth:** Marty Cagan / Melissa Perri ↔ Elena Verna / Andrew Chen.
- **Quant vs qual:** Ronny Kohavi (experimentation) ↔ Jakob Nielsen / Don Norman (usability, humane design).
- **Minimalism vs maximalism:** Dieter Rams / Jony Ive ↔ Paula Scher.
- **AI-will-fix-a11y vs accessibility-as-craft:** Jakob Nielsen ↔ Léonie Watson.
