## The Design Sprint — origin and structure (verified, GV)

From https://www.gv.com/sprint/:
- Developed at **GV (Google Ventures)**. Knapp began running sprints at Google in **2010** (Chrome, Google Search, Google X). In **2012** he brought it to GV, where the team refined it.
- Earlier, Knapp designed **Gmail's Priority Inbox** (an early AI-product design, per character.vc copy) and co-founded **Google Meet / Google Hangouts**.
- Five-day weekly structure:
  - **Monday** — Map the problem; pick a target.
  - **Tuesday** — Sketch competing solutions on paper.
  - **Wednesday** — Decide; build a testable storyboard/hypothesis.
  - **Thursday** — Build a realistic (high-fidelity) prototype.
  - **Friday** — Test with 5 real customers.
- Notable sprint clients: Slack, Blue Bottle Coffee, FitStar, Savioke, Foundation Medicine, Nest, Medium, Gusto, Flatiron Health, Uber.
- Ev Williams (Medium/Twitter founder) blurb: "Read this book and do what it says if you want to build better products faster."

## The Foundation Sprint — the key 2025–2026 method (verified)

Sources:
- https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/introducing-the-foundation-sprint (published **January 28, 2025**)
- https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/the-foundation-sprint-jake-knapp-and-john-zeratsky (podcast, published **July 13, 2025**)
- https://thefoundationsprint.com/ (workshop site; references *Click*, training May 20–21, 2025)
- https://www.kreativ-reaktor.de/en/post/foundation-sprint-the-strategic-foundation-for-digital-innovations-in-the-ai-age ("Foundation Sprint 2026: Strategy Over AI Speed")

Mechanics:
- **2-day workshop** that *precedes* the Design Sprint. Compresses ~3–6 months / "6 months of strategy" into 2 days.
- **Day 1 — Define & Differentiate (The Basics):** identify the real customer, clarify the exact problem, map competitors and alternatives, define unique advantages via a **differentiation 2x2**.
- **Day 2 — Find the Right Approach:** explore delivery paths and apply the **Magic Lenses** framework to evaluate/stress-test ideas and choose the strongest path.
- **Outcome — the Founding Hypothesis:** a single structured, testable statement combining (target customer) + (problem solved) + (competitive differentiation). Knapp's words: *"a handy Mad Libs–style sentence that we can fill in to understand and test a team's strategy."* (Lenny's, Jan 28, 2025)
- Distinction: Design Sprint solves a *defined* problem via prototype + test; Foundation Sprint answers the earlier question — *are you building the right thing for the right person, before you commit resources?*
