## Canonical works & key writing (verified URLs)

- **"Growth Loops are the New Funnels"** — Reforge, co-authored with Brian Balfour, Kevin Kwok, Andrew Chen (2018). https://www.reforge.com/blog/growth-loops
  - Core claims: funnels are too micro; they create org silos (marketing owns acquisition, product owns retention) that optimize at each other's expense; funnels have no compounding/reinvestment of outputs back into inputs. Growth loops capture how the fastest-growing products actually grow.
- **"How to build and scale winning marketplaces"** (First Round Review podcast/essay).
  - https://review.firstround.com/podcast/building-winning-marketplaces-casey-winters/
  - "The product in a marketplace is the supply, not your software." "The only thing supply cares about is more demand." Three marketplace prerequisites: supply fragmentation, transaction frequency / buyer promiscuity, and a sustainable acquisition advantage (acquisition moat). Setup → Aha → Habit activation framework. Don't expand to many markets without a refined expansion playbook.
- **"Pinterest and Grubhub's Former Growth Lead on Building Content Loops"** (First Round Review).
  - https://review.firstround.com/pinterest-and-grubhubs-former-growth-lead-on-building-content-loops/
  - Four acquisition-loop types: viral loops, paid-acquisition loops, sales loops, content loops. Most new-customer acquisition comes from retained customers via these loops (they talk/invite, create shareable content, or fund paid acquisition).
- **"What Is Good Retention: An Exhaustive Benchmark Study"** — with Lenny Rachitsky.
  - https://caseyaccidental.com/what-is-good-retention
  - Retention benchmarks per business model; three levers to improve retention: (1) make the product more valuable (feature/product fit), (2) connect users to existing value (onboarding), (3) referral/resurrection.
- **"When and How to Build Second Products"** — caseyaccidental.com.
  - https://www.caseyaccidental.com/p/second-products
  - "New products work when they have to. If you can still grow your core product, they rarely get the focus they need to succeed." Second products don't need full PMF — they only need to influence one of acquisition/retention/monetization meaningfully. Pinterest vs Snapchat, Figma vs Canva case studies.
- **"Why Onboarding is the Most Crucial Part of Your Growth Strategy"** — Medium.
  - https://medium.com/@onecaseman/why-onboarding-is-the-most-crucial-part-of-your-growth-strategy-8f9ad3ec8d5e
- **Casey Accidental blog / Substack** — home of his writing. https://www.caseyaccidental.com/

## Documented stances & quotes (for public_stances, each carries an evidence_url)

- Retention is the foundation of all sustainable growth; "retention really is growth's triple word score"; it is "the most important and least understood" phase of startup growth. (caseyaccidental retention tag; Reforge/Intercom interview.)
  - https://caseyaccidental.com/tag/retention/
  - https://blog.intercom.com/greylocks-casey-winters-on-how-to-create-meaningful-growth/
- Growth loops, not funnels, are the correct model for sustainable growth. (Reforge "Growth Loops are the New Funnels".)
  - https://www.reforge.com/blog/growth-loops
- In a marketplace, "the product is the supply, not your software"; demand is the constraint suppliers care about; you need a sustainable acquisition advantage. (First Round marketplaces.)
  - https://review.firstround.com/podcast/building-winning-marketplaces-casey-winters/
- Most new-customer acquisition is powered by retained customers through a small number of acquisition loops. (First Round content loops.)
  - https://review.firstround.com/pinterest-and-grubhubs-former-growth-lead-on-building-content-loops/
- Product/market fit is "retention that allows for sustained growth"; bake the growth loop into the PMF hypothesis, don't bolt it on later. (caseyaccidental.)
  - https://www.caseyaccidental.com/about
- New products should be built only when the core product can no longer grow — "new products work when they have to." (Second Products essay.)
  - https://www.caseyaccidental.com/p/second-products
- AI profiles scale access to expert *judgment* and *reasoning*, not finished answers; they augment, not replace, humans. (SuperMe launch; founder-journey post.)
  - https://www.caseyaccidental.com/p/talking-my-founder-journey-so-far
