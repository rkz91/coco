## Verbatim quotes (with sources)

### On AI vs. judgment — "This Competition Exposed How AI is Reshaping Design" (2025-03-07)
- "Design is deciding." / "The best designers are the best deciders."
- "AI doesn't have that problem. It just produces."
- "It's diffusion. It's pattern matching. It's statistical synthesis and averaging."
- "But let's not conflate that with judgment, or even selection."
- "designers decide whether to follow patterns or diverge from them. Generative AI doesn't yet consciously do the latter unless instructed to."
- "without the muscle memory of knowing what to make and when, they plateau easily."

### On what NOT to do — On Theme podcast (2025-02-10)
- "The most successful design systems are specific and not generic."
- "If you try to design something applicable to anything, you can't do one thing really well."
- "Let chaos rein, then rein in chaos." (Andy Grove framing applied to design systems.)
- Tokens are an "advanced level concept," not a basic one — retrofit later.
- The one-click-rebrand promise of tokens is largely theoretical; an org spent 6–12 months on tokens, then would need another ~6 months to actually rebrand.

### On collaboration — "The Hot Potato Process" (2019-10-13)
- "Designers hand off comps to developers and think their work is done. That puts a lot of pressure on the designer to get everything perfect in one pass."
- "Working together means working together."
- Design and development should be "interwoven rather than sequential."
- Best way to start: designer and developer "sit together."

### On the agency sale — "I Sold My Agency SuperFriendly" (2025-03-03)
- "for the first time in a decade, I think I'm running out of things that I want to learn or try through this vehicle."
- "the skills and vision that got it here aren't the same ones needed to take it forward."

### On pricing/value — "You're Reading the Signals Wrong" (2026-05-01) and Fractional by Swarm
- "It's a math problem, not a market problem."
- "If you don't know what your work is actually worth, you can't price it well, which means you can't pitch it well."
- "I typically recommend pricing your projects at around 10% of the value you create."
- "You're not cheaper as a freelancer. You're more valuable."
- "I want the power dynamic to be in favor of the service provider."

### On the design system as a practice — Design That Scales (2023)
- Core thesis: most design system initiatives fail because they are treated as technical artifacts rather than sustained organizational practices.
- "a design system is crucial for any organization managing two or more digital products."
- Chapter 5: "Pilots — The Best Way to Start and Sustain a Design System."
