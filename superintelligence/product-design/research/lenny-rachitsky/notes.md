## 1. Identity and career timeline (high confidence)

- **Full name:** Lenny Rachitsky. Handle `@lennysan` (X/Twitter), `@lenny` (Substack).
- **Localmind:** Founded 2011 as CEO — a location-based Q&A / recommendation platform for travelers. Acquired by Airbnb in **2012**.
  - Source: <https://airfocus.com/product-heroes/lenny-rachitsky/>
- **Airbnb:** ~7 years (2012–2019). Joined via the Localmind acquisition, moved from engineering into product.
  - 2012: PM for guest conversion / host quality.
  - 2016: promoted to **Product Lead, Supply Growth** — managed 5 PMs and oversaw an 80+ person cross-functional team driving consumer supply growth.
  - Led a ~2.5-year initiative to shift Airbnb's marketplace toward **instant bookings** (from ~5% of bookings at the outset) against significant internal resistance.
  - Sources: <https://airfocus.com/product-heroes/lenny-rachitsky/>, <https://productschool.com/product-leaders/lenny-rachitsky>
- **Lenny's Newsletter:** launched on Substack in **2019** (post-Airbnb). Now consistently a top business/Substack newsletter.
- **Lenny's Podcast:** launched **June 2022**, three years after the newsletter. Releases ~two episodes/week, interviewing product/growth leaders.
  - Source: <https://www.cnbc.com/2024/01/11/lenny-rachitsky-podcast-brings-in-more-than-500k-a-year-heres-how.html>
- **Now:** writer, angel investor, product/growth advisor. Runs the newsletter, podcast, the "How I AI" series, Lenny's Product Pass, and the Lenny & Friends Summit.
