## Biography / career timeline

- **Alarm.com** — early engineering role.
- **Jet.com** — engineer (Jet.com later acquired by Walmart).
- **Netflix** — Senior Software Engineer on the Chaos Engineering / resilience team. Keynoted **AWS re:Invent November 2017** to help launch the chaos-engineering movement into the enterprise mainstream.
- **Slack** — Senior Software Engineer, reliability / chaos engineering.
- **Learning From Incidents (LFI)** — founded the community ~2019 with a few Netflix colleagues; runs learningfromincidents.io plus an open Slack workspace; focuses on resilience engineering, human factors, systems safety.
- **Jeli, Inc.** — founder and CEO, 2019–2023. First dedicated incident-analysis platform. Public launch Dec 2020 ($4M). Acquired by PagerDuty Nov 2023.
- **PagerDuty** — Nov 2023 onward. Currently Senior Director, Head of Pricing and Product Strategy and Growth.
