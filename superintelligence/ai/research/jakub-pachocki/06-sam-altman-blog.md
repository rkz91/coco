# Sam Altman blog — "Jakub and Szymon"

Source: https://blog.samaltman.com/jakub-and-szymon
Retrieved: 2026-05-27 (post likely from May 2024 around Sutskever transition)

## Pachocki's role per Altman

- Chief Scientist at OpenAI.

## Contributions attributed to Pachocki

- Collaborated with Szymon Sidor to scale up reinforcement learning, leading to OpenAI's Dota 2 result.
- Built much of the infrastructure enabling scientific discoveries.
- Led GPT-4 pretraining efforts.
- Drove initial ideas (with Ilya Sutskever and Lukasz Kaiser) that led to reasoning breakthroughs.
- Made significant progress exploring new paradigms.

## Altman's characterisation

- "OpenAI has not yet thrown a problem at them they have not been able to solve."
- Describes the Pachocki + Sidor partnership as comparable to "research labs of the past where two people are able to complement each other so well."

## Pachocki quote (about Sidor)

- "indefatigable" — Altman: "as perfect of a use of that word as I have ever heard."

## Other public Altman quote on Pachocki

- "easily one of the greatest minds of our generation." (Reported in Wikipedia and TIME100 AI 2025.)
