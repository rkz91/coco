# Wikipedia — Jakub Pachocki

Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jakub_Pachocki
Retrieved: 2026-05-27

## Personal Information

- Born: 1991 (age 34–35) in Gdansk, Poland
- Nationality: Polish
- Known alias in competitive programming: meret

## Education

- Undergraduate: Computer Science, University of Warsaw
- Graduate: PhD in Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University (2016)
  - Advisor: Gary Miller
  - Thesis: "Graphs and Beyond: Faster Algorithms for High Dimensional Convex Optimization"

## Career Timeline

### Early Career

- 2011-2012: Software engineering intern at Facebook
- Post-PhD: Postdoctoral researcher at Harvard University and Simons Institute for the Theory of Computing

### OpenAI (2017–Present)

- 2017: Joined OpenAI
- 2021: Became Research Director, leading development of GPT-4 and OpenAI Five
- May 2024: Promoted to Chief Scientist following Ilya Sutskever's departure

## Key Projects & Contributions

- Led GPT-4 development as Research Director
- Oversaw OpenAI Five (competitive gaming AI for Dota 2)
- Current involvement with reasoning models (o1/o3 projects via the "Strawberry" initiative)

## Recognition

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman stated Pachocki is "easily one of the greatest minds of our generation."

## Competitive Programming Achievements

- International Olympiad in Informatics: Silver medal (2009)
- ICPC World Finals: Gold medal, 2nd place overall (2012)
- Google Code Jam: Champion (2012), Third place (2011)
- Facebook Hacker Cup: Second place (2013)
- TopCoder Open Algorithm: Second place (2012)
