## the-decoder.com coverage of Nature interview (2025-05)

Source: https://the-decoder.com/openais-chief-scientist-jakub-pachocki-says-there-is-evidence-that-ai-models-discover-novel-insights/

Pachocki's direct quotes (sourced from the Nature May 12, 2025 interview):

- "I would say it is a form of reasoning, but that doesn't mean it's the same as how humans reason."
- "I definitely believe we have significant evidence that the models are capable of discovering novel insights."
- "Reasoning models don't learn how to think in a vacuum, they are rooted in a model that has learned through pre-training."
- "This for me is closest to what I previously emotionally thought of as AGI."
- He expects "substantial progress" in autonomous AI research by the end of the decade.
- First practical applications (autonomous software creation) may arrive in 2025.
