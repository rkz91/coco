# MIT Tech Review — "The two people shaping the future of OpenAI's research"

Source: https://www.technologyreview.com/2025/07/31/1120885/the-two-people-shaping-the-future-of-openais-research/
Published: 2025-07-31
Subjects: Jakub Pachocki (Chief Scientist) and Mark Chen (Chief Research Officer)

## Pachocki's role definition

- "I am responsible for setting the research roadmap and establishing our long-term technical vision."
- Joined OpenAI in 2017 from academic theoretical computer science.
- Replaced Ilya Sutskever as chief scientist in May 2024.
- Co-architect of OpenAI's reasoning models, particularly o1 and o3.

## Key direct quotes

- "I think that's quite underrated" (on competitive programming performance as a signal of research talent).
- "There's fluidity in the roles. We're both researchers, we pull on technical threads."
- "We are probably still, like, at the very beginning of this reasoning paradigm."
- "Really, we are thinking about how to get these models to learn and explore over the long term."
- "Two years ago the risks that we were imagining were mostly theoretical risks."
- "The world today looks very different, and I think a lot of alignment problems are now very practically motivated."
- "I am often afforded the luxury of really kind of thinking about the long term, where the technology is headed."
- "When computers can develop new technologies themselves seems like a very important, um, inflection point."
- "Psyho spent half the programming competition thinking and then came up with a solution that was really novel and quite different from anything that our model looked at. This is really what we're after."

## Research vision

Pachocki frames AI progress around:

- Reasoning paradigm development: breaking complex problems into manageable steps.
- Long-horizon autonomous research: models working independently on extended projects.
- Knowledge synthesis: connecting insights across domains rather than isolated problem-solving.
- Scientific acceleration: using AI to advance human knowledge rather than replace human intelligence.

## Leadership style

Pachocki focuses on long-term technical direction while Chen manages operational realities.

## Alignment team

- Superalignment team (originally co-led by Sutskever) has dissolved.
- Pachocki: alignment is "part of the core business rather than the concern of one specific team."
