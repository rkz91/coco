# Prafulla Dhariwal — Biography and Role

Source notes captured 2026-05-27 from multiple web sources.

## Identity

- **Full name:** Prafulla Susil Dhariwal
- **Origin:** Pune, India. Attended Dr. Kalmadi Shamrao High School.
- **Education:** Massachusetts Institute of Technology, undergraduate degree 2017 (computers, math, physics double/triple).
- **Current residence:** San Francisco, California.
- **Twitter / X handle:** `@prafdhar` (confirmed active, low-volume account).
- **Personal site:** https://prafulladhariwal.com/

## Career trajectory at OpenAI

- **May 2016:** Joined OpenAI as a research intern while still at MIT.
- **2017:** Full-time research scientist after MIT graduation.
- **2024:** Identified as **Head of the "Omni" team** (multimodal) at OpenAI. The Omni team produced GPT-4o.
- **September 2025:** Promoted to **Technical Fellow at OpenAI** — one of the company's top individual contributor titles. Confirmed via ContactOut and AD Scientific Index profiles, both dated 2025.
- **2026:** Continues at OpenAI as Technical Fellow with multimodal responsibilities even after Sora app was discontinued (March 24, 2026 announcement; April 26, 2026 app shutdown).

## Role disambiguation (important)

Multiple Indian-press articles describe Dhariwal as "the brain behind Sora" or "Sora's technical lead." This is **partly inaccurate**: Wikipedia and OpenAI's own credits identify **Tim Brooks and Bill Peebles** as the Sora research leads (Brooks later moved to Google DeepMind). What is true and well-sourced:

- Dhariwal led the **Omni team that produced GPT-4o**, including the 4o image generation system released March 2025.
- Dhariwal is named in the **special thanks** of the Sora 2 announcement (September 30, 2025), indicating advisory / cross-team contribution but not lead authorship.
- His **canonical contribution to the field** is the diffusion modeling foundations — "Diffusion Models Beat GANs on Image Synthesis" (NeurIPS 2021) and classifier guidance — which underpin Sora as well as every other modern diffusion-based generative system at OpenAI.

Profile should describe him as "Omni / multimodal team lead, foundational diffusion researcher whose 2021 work underpins Sora and GPT-4o image generation" — not as "Sora team lead."

## Sam Altman attribution

After GPT-4o launched in May 2024, Sam Altman publicly tweeted:

> "GPT-4o would not have happened without the vision, talent, conviction, and determination of @prafdhar (Prafulla Dhariwal) over a long period of time. That (along with the work of many others) led to what I hope will turn out to be a revolution in how we use computers."

This is the most-quoted external recognition of Dhariwal's leadership role.
