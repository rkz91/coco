## Notes on source reliability

- **OpenAI announcements:** authoritative for what was shipped and who was credited.
- **Indian press:** authoritative for biographical details but unreliable on technical role (consistently overclaims Dhariwal as "Sora lead" when he was actually Omni / multimodal lead with foundational diffusion work that underpins Sora; Tim Brooks and Bill Peebles were the named Sora research leads).
- **prafulladhariwal.com:** Dhariwal's own site is the most reliable for publication list; not updated frequently.
- **Olympiad records:** authoritative.
- **Twitter @prafdhar:** authoritative for what he chose to communicate publicly, which is sparse.
