## Significance

Dhariwal is one of the rare individuals with **medals across three different International Science Olympiads** (Math, Physics, Astronomy) plus IOI selection. The Indian press emphasizes this prodigy framing heavily in 2024–2025 coverage. The cluster of olympiad wins from 2011–2013 places his high school years at Dr. Kalmadi Shamrao High School in Pune.

## How this shapes his profile

The olympiad background suggests:
1. Strong mathematical-physical foundations — visible in his preference for variational / probabilistic framings over purely empirical approaches in diffusion work.
2. Comfort with abstraction at scale — diffusion models, normalizing flows (Glow), and consistency models all sit at the intersection of probability theory and engineering.
3. Quiet, individual-contributor pattern matching olympiad culture: the work speaks; the person does not promote.
