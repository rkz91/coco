# Aravind Srinivas — Biography and Education

Compiled 2026-05-27 from public sources.

## Identity

- Full name: Aravind Srinivas
- Born: ~1993/1994 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India
- Nationality: Indian
- Current location: San Francisco Bay Area, USA
- Current role: Co-founder and CEO, Perplexity AI

## Education

- **IIT Madras** — Dual degree, Electrical Engineering (Bachelor + Master), graduated 2017
- **UC Berkeley** — PhD, Computer Science, conferred 2021 (he is referenced as "PhD '21" in Haas News). PhD advisor: Pieter Abbeel. Worked closely with Berkeley Robot Learning Lab.
- Pre-PhD research orientation: deep reinforcement learning and self-supervised representation learning for vision.

## Research career before Perplexity

He held research roles at three top AI labs while doing his PhD:

- **OpenAI** — research intern
- **Google Brain** — research intern
- **DeepMind** — research intern

These stints exposed him to large language models, reinforcement learning, and generative model pipelines that later informed Perplexity's architecture (foundation-model-on-top-of-search-index pattern).

## Founding Perplexity

- **Founded:** August 2022
- **Co-founders:**
  - Denis Yarats (CTO; ex-Facebook AI Research)
  - Johnny Ho (Chief Strategy Officer; ex-Quora)
  - Andy Konwinski (engineer; ex-Databricks co-founder)
- **First product launched:** December 7, 2022 — the Perplexity answer-engine.
- Initial pitch: an "answer engine, not a search engine." Direct, sourced answers with inline citations.

## Wealth and recognition

- TIME 100 Most Influential People in AI, 2024.
- Debuted on M3M Hurun India Rich List, October 2025, with an estimated net worth of approximately ₹211 billion (~$2.5 billion at the time), making him India's youngest billionaire at age 31.
