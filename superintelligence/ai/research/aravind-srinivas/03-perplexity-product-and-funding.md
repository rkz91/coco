## Apple acquisition rumors

- **Jun 2025:** Bloomberg reports Apple executives Adrian Perica (M&A), Eddy Cue (Services), and AI leadership held internal talks about acquiring Perplexity. Talks called "early stage."
- **Aug 2025:** Apple is still debating Perplexity vs. Mistral, with Cue most vocal in favor and Craig Federighi most hesitant (believes Apple's internal AI team can close the gap).
- **Sep 2025:** Apple announces World Knowledge Answers — its own Perplexity/ChatGPT-style answer feature inside Siri — for early 2026 launch. Analysts (Wedbush) called Apple acquiring Perplexity a "no-brainer" at ~$30B.
- **Late 2025/2026:** Deal effectively dead. Apple chose to build, not buy.

## Plagiarism / publisher controversies (2024)

- **Wired investigation (June 2024):** Perplexity allegedly crawled sites that had disallowed crawling in robots.txt. Srinivas attributed this to a third-party crawler not owned by Perplexity.
- **Forbes (June 2024):** Perplexity Pages reproduced text and imagery from a Forbes article on Eric Schmidt nearly verbatim. Srinivas called it "rough edges." Forbes sent cease-and-desist.
- **News Corp / Dow Jones / NY Post (Oct 2024):** Lawsuit alleging "content kleptocracy."
- **NY Times:** Cease-and-desist letter.
- **TechCrunch Disrupt 2024 (Oct):** Srinivas would not give a clean definition of plagiarism on stage.
- **Resolution path:** Revenue-share program (Time, Fortune, Der Spiegel, Adweek, Gannett, Lee Enterprises, The Independent, Texas Tribune) launched 2024–2025. Comet Plus (2025) generalized this to $42.5M payout pool with 80/20 split for CNN, Condé Nast, Fortune, Le Figaro, Le Monde, LA Times, Washington Post.
