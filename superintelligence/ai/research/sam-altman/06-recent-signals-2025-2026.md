## Pattern Summary

Three themes run through every 2025-2026 signal:
1. **Compute and infrastructure are the moat.** Every public communication leans into Stargate, gigawatts, energy, capital.
2. **AGI/superintelligence timelines stay aggressive but the framing softens.** "Few thousand days" survives; "jobs apocalypse" doesn't.
3. **Governance moves are completed, then defended.** PBC conversion → Musk lawsuit → board reconstitution → all settle in a configuration that consolidates Altman's operational control.
