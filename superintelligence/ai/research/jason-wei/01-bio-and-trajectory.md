## Why his trajectory matters for the panel

Jason Wei is one of a small cohort of researchers (with Hyung Won Chung, Barret Zoph, Denny Zhou) whose careers thread Google Brain → OpenAI → Anthropic, mirroring the gravitational shift in post-training talent toward Anthropic in 2025. His moves are signal: when Wei moves, it indicates where the post-training research frontier is concentrating.

He is also the originator (or co-originator) of three of the most-cited prompting / scaling techniques in the LLM era:
1. **Chain-of-Thought prompting** — NeurIPS 2022, ~13,000+ citations as of 2026
2. **Emergent abilities of LLMs** — TMLR 2022, the canonical "phase transitions in capability" paper
3. **Instruction tuning** — FLAN, ICLR 2022, the bridge between pretraining and "usefulness"
