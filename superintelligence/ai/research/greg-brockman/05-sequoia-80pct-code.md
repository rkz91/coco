# Sequoia AI Ascent 2026 — "80% of OpenAI's code is now written by AI" (2026-05-01)

Source: https://thenextweb.com/news/openai-brockman-80-percent-code-ai-productivity-claim

## Headline quote
At Sequoia Capital's AI Ascent 2026 conference, Brockman characterized current AI-assisted engineering productivity:
- "It's hard to know what percent is not being written by AI" (regarding the 80% figure)

## On the December 2025 inflection
- Productivity shift in December 2025: models moved from handling ~20% of typical engineering tasks to ~80%
- "You absolutely need to retool your workflow around these AIs."

## On jagged capability
- "The technology we have right now is very jagged. It is absolutely superhuman at many tasks. When it comes to writing code, those kinds of things, the AI can just do it. But there's some very basic tasks that a human can do that our AI still struggles with."

## Example workflow
- Cited an OpenAI engineer who provides design documents to AI models for complete implementation, instrumentation, and profiling to production quality.

## Strategic significance
- Brockman cited this productivity shift to justify OpenAI's $122B capital deployment toward compute infrastructure.
