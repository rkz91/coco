# Fortune — "Meet the power broker of the AI age" (2025-11-05)

Source: https://fortune.com/2025/11/05/openai-greg-brockman-ai-infrastructure-data-center-master-builder/

## Title and role
- "Builder-in-chief" of OpenAI; high-visibility operator executing Altman's vision
- Leading Stargate Project — $500B initial, $1.4T total committed; ~30 GW of compute capacity

## Direct quotes from Brockman
- On AGI as engineering: "Building AGI as an end-to-end engineering challenge, one that spans everything from how the models are designed to the chips, servers, and data centers that power the training and running of models."
- On scaling laws: "There's no bend in the scaling laws... The thing that's hard is execution."
- On the inflection point: "At that point, you need data centers" (looking back at 2017 projections that required $10B in hardware)
- On the Scaling team: it "merged the deep learning engineering of both our research and applied teams" with the mandate to "make sure we have (and can maximally harness) the computing power we need to train and run our models."
- Vision for compute: "Imagine it just thinking about how to solve a Millennium Problem or how to cure a specific kind of cancer... That level of computational power coupled with the ability to experiment and learn from your ideas, that is going to be something the world has never seen."
- On compute economics: "If we had 10 times more compute, I don't know if we'd have 10 times more revenue, but I don't think we would be that far."
