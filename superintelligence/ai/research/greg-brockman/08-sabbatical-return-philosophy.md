# Sabbatical, return, and engineering philosophy

## Sabbatical — August 2024
Source: https://x.com/gdb/status/1820644694264791459

Tweet from @gdb, August 5, 2024:
"I'm taking a sabbatical through end of year. First time to relax since co-founding OpenAI 9 years ago. The mission is far from complete; we still have a safe AGI to build."

Context: media reporting suggested he and Altman agreed on the sabbatical amid concerns his demanding leadership style had created team tension.

## Return — November 12, 2024
Posted "Back to building OpenAI"; focus on "key technical challenges."
Created a new internal team called **Scaling**, merging deep-learning engineering from research and applied groups.
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greg_Brockman, https://fortune.com/2025/11/05/openai-greg-brockman-ai-infrastructure-data-center-master-builder/

## "The engineer's engineer" framing
Source: https://digidai.github.io/2025/11/28/greg-brockman-openai-cofounder-president-builder-chief-deep-analysis/

- Known within Silicon Valley as "the engineer's engineer"
- Sees OpenAI as a systems problem, not just a research lab
- Approximately 80% of his time on hands-on coding even as president (pre-sabbatical)
- "Technical leadership should call the shots while still doing hands-on technical work"

## Belief statements (Sharon Goldman piece)
Source: https://sharongoldman.substack.com/p/why-i-really-pushed-to-talk-to-openais

- On AGI: "The fundamental bet is that AGI is possible, and if we are right about that, then it will really change everything."
- On progress: "In my mind, the real question is, do you believe in continued AI progress?"
- Described as historically "putting his head down and coding software for days on end"
- Characterized as both "builder and believer" — the "secret sauce" behind OpenAI's major infrastructure partnerships (Nvidia, AMD, Broadcom, Oracle, Amazon)

## Public statements on AI capability and responsibility
From Lisa Su Bloomberg interview + multiple 2025 talks:
- "AI is almost like a rocket ship for the mind. We are able to go places, solve problems that are totally impossible."
- "This technology is so desirable, it will be built with or without us. But what we can do is help steer it, is help decide how we want this to integrate with society."
- Treats compute as "the currency of intelligence"

## November 2023 crisis behaviour
- Removed as board chair simultaneously with Altman firing (November 16, 2023)
- Resigned immediately: "based on today's news, i quit"
- Briefly accepted Microsoft role with Altman (Nov 20)
- Returned to OpenAI Nov 21 with Altman's reinstatement
- Relationship with Ilya Sutskever reportedly strained; Sutskever later left to found Safe Superintelligence
