# Stargate announcement + AMD partnership (2025)

## Stargate Project — announced January 21, 2025
Source: https://openai.com/index/announcing-the-stargate-project/ (HTTP 403 on direct fetch, confirmed via multiple secondary sources)
Secondary: https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/openai-announces-five-more-us-stargate-data-centers-with-oracle-and-softbank/

- $500B over 4 years to build AI infrastructure for OpenAI in the US
- $100B begins deploying immediately
- Initial equity funders: SoftBank, OpenAI, Oracle, MGX
- SoftBank lead on financial responsibility; OpenAI lead on operational responsibility
- Announced January 21, 2025 at White House with Trump, Altman, Masayoshi Son (SoftBank), Larry Ellison (Oracle)
- Greg Brockman is the operational lead executing the buildout

## AMD strategic partnership — October 6, 2025
Source: https://openai.com/index/openai-amd-strategic-partnership/

- 6 gigawatts of AMD GPUs across multiple AMD Instinct generations
- Largest GPU deployment AMD has ever announced

### Brockman quote (October 6, 2025)
"Building the future of AI requires deep collaboration across every layer of the stack. Working alongside AMD will allow us to scale to deliver AI tools that benefit people everywhere."

### Compute scarcity framing
"We're in a position where we cannot launch features. We cannot launch new products simply because of lack of computational power... we're heading to a world by default that looks like a compute desert."
