# OpenAI reorganization — Brockman gets product control (2026-05-15)

Source: https://www.implicator.ai/greg-brockman-gets-product-control-as-openai-folds-around-codex/
Companion: https://thenextweb.com/news/openai-brockman-chatgpt-codex-unified-agentic-platform

## What changed
- Greg Brockman now officially runs product strategy in addition to infrastructure
- Previously held on interim basis during Fidji Simo's medical leave
- OpenAI consolidating ChatGPT, Codex, and developer API into one unified core product team
- Codex used by 4M+ people weekly

## New org structure
- **Thibault Sottiaux** (former Codex eng lead) — core product and platform
- **Nick Turley** (ChatGPT) — enterprise products
- **Ashley Alexander** (former Instagram VP) — consumer product

## Brockman memo quotes
- "We're consolidating our product efforts"
- Plans to "invest in a single agentic platform"

## Strategic frame
The reorganization concentrates product power around an agent-based architecture and signals that OpenAI sees a single agentic experience (Codex + ChatGPT unified) as the next form factor. Brockman is the executive responsible for delivering it.
