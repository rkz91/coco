## Marble — first commercial product (launched November 12, 2025)

### What it is
Marble converts text prompts, photos, videos, 3D layouts, or 360 panoramas into editable, downloadable 3D environments. Available freemium and paid tiers.

### Differentiator vs. competitors (Odyssey, Decart, Google's Genie)
Marble creates **persistent, downloadable** 3D worlds rather than generating environments dynamically during exploration. Reduces morphing artifacts. Exports as Gaussian splats, meshes, or videos.

### Chisel — the AI-native 3D editor
Hybrid editor lets users **block out spatial structures before AI fills visual details**. Co-founder Justin Johnson framed this as "separating structure from style like HTML and CSS." Theme repeatedly cited by Li: "creative control."

### Subscription tiers
- Free — 4 generations
- Standard — $20/month, 12 generations, multi-image input
- Pro — $35/month, 25 generations, scene expansion, commercial rights
- Max — $95/month, all features, 75 generations

### Intended applications
Gaming, visual effects, virtual reality, robotics training simulations.
