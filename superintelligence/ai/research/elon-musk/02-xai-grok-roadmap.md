## Model timeline

- Grok 1 — November 2023, open-weights release
- Grok 2 — August 2024
- Grok 3 — February 2025
- Grok 4 — Summer 2025
- Grok 4.20 — late 2025 transitional release
- Grok 5 — initially targeted late 2025, slipped to Q1 2026, then Q2 2026 per Grok's own X account update Feb 25 2026

## Grok 5 specifications

- 6 trillion parameters total (Mixture-of-Experts) — confirmed by Musk at Baron Capital conference November 2025
- Some sources cite 10T parameter target — likely encompasses full MoE expert count vs active
- Training on Colossus 2
- Musk: "10% chance Grok 5 reaches human-level intelligence" — researcher community broadly skeptical
- No official benchmarks yet (still in training as of May 2026)

## Training infrastructure positioning

xAI strategy: vertically integrate compute (Colossus), model (Grok), distribution (X platform native integration), and downstream agents. Pattern echoes Tesla's vertical integration: silicon (Dojo / FSD chip) + manufacturing + product + data feedback loop.

## Brand / framing claims

- Grok marketed as "maximally truth-seeking" and "maximally curious"
- Branded as the "based" alternative to OpenAI and Anthropic's "woke" alignment
- Pitched as native X-platform AI with real-time access to the social graph
- Has had multiple high-profile safety incidents (see 06-grok-safety-incidents.md)
