# Elon Musk — Wikipedia biography extract

Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elon_Musk
Retrieved: 2026-05-28

## Personal

- Full legal name: Elon Reeve Musk
- Born June 28, 1971 in Pretoria, South Africa
- Citizenships: South African (birth), Canadian (via mother), American (1995, naturalized 2002)

## Education

- Briefly enrolled at University of Pretoria
- Queen's University (Kingston, Ontario) — transferred to U Penn
- University of Pennsylvania — Wharton School: BA Physics (1997) + BS Economics (1997)
- Stanford PhD applied physics — admitted 1995, dropped out within two days to launch Zip2

(Note: Karpathy-style profiles tend to note "Stanford PhD applied physics, dropped out 1995" — this is the canonical framing.)

## Current 2025-2026 roles

- Tesla — CEO and product architect (joined as early investor 2004; CEO since 2008)
- SpaceX — Founder, CEO, and chief engineer (founded 2002)
- xAI — Founder and CEO (founded July 2023; became a SpaceX subsidiary in 2026)
- X Corp — Founder and CTO (acquired Twitter October 2022 for $44B; rebranded X 2023)
- Neuralink — CEO (co-founder 2016)
- The Boring Company — Founder
- America Party — Founder (announced July 5, 2025)

## US Government role

- Senior Advisor to the President, January 20 – May 30, 2025
- De facto head of DOGE (Department of Government Efficiency)
- Exit at end of May 2025 after legal setbacks and Cabinet clashes
- DOGE dissolved as a centralized entity November 2025 (Scott Kupor / OPM statement)

## OpenAI history

- Co-founded December 2015 alongside Sam Altman, Ilya Sutskever, Greg Brockman, others
- Initial donation of $44M to OpenAI nonprofit
- Departed board February 2018 citing conflict of interest with Tesla AI work
- Filed Musk v. Altman February 29, 2024 in San Francisco Superior Court
- Refiled in US District Court Northern District of California August 5, 2024
- Lawsuit dismissed May 18, 2026 on statute-of-limitations grounds (Judge Yvonne Gonzalez Rogers)
- Musk vowed to appeal to 9th Circuit

## Tesla $1T pay package

- November 2025: Tesla shareholders approved a compensation package worth ~$1 trillion over 10 years contingent on meeting specific market-cap and product milestones
- Replaces the 2018 package that was twice voided by Delaware Chancery Court

## Net worth

- Wealthiest person in the world since 2025
- ~$828B (Forbes, May 2026)

## Political shift

- $250M+ to Trump 2024 campaign
- Senior Advisor January-May 2025
- Public split over "One Big Beautiful Bill Act" June-July 2025
- Called the bill a "disgusting abomination" and "DEBT SLAVERY"
- Formed America Party July 5, 2025
- As of late 2025-early 2026: "politically homeless" framing; opposing both major parties' fiscal posture; still aligned with Trump administration on AI regulation despite personal feud
