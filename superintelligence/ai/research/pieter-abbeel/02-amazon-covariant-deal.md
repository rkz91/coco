## Phase 1 — August 30, 2024

Amazon announced on a Friday evening (just before Labor Day weekend) that it had:

- Hired Covariant's founders (Pieter Abbeel, Peter Chen, Rocky Duan) plus "about a quarter" of staff.
- Signed a non-exclusive license to Covariant's robotic foundation models (RFM-1 lineage).
- Joined the founders to **Amazon Fulfillment Technologies & Robotics Team**.

Joseph Quinlivan, VP Amazon Fulfillment Technologies & Robotics: *"Embedding Covariant's AI technology into our existing robot fleet will make them more performant and create real world value."*

Deal structure characterized as a "reverse acquihire" — a workaround for antitrust scrutiny on outright tech acquisitions. Functional outcome equivalent to acquisition; legally a hire + license.

## Financial details (whistleblower disclosure, 2025)

A 2025 whistleblower complaint filed with FTC, SEC, and DOJ revealed:
- Licensing fee: **$380 million**
- Deferred licensing payment: **$20 million** one year after close
- Pre-deal 2023 valuation: $625M (Series C)
- Remaining Covariant entity continues under Ted Stinson (CEO, formerly COO) and Tianhao Zhang. Characterized in the complaint as a "zombie company."

## Phase 2 — December 17, 2025

Andy Jassy memo announced major AGI reorg:
- **Rohit Prasad out** as Amazon AI chief.
- **Peter DeSantis** (27-year Amazon veteran, former SVP Utility Computing) named to lead the AGI organization. AGI reports directly to Jassy.
- **Pieter Abbeel** named head of the **frontier model research team** — "the base model builders" — within AGI. Continues working with the robotics team in parallel.

The Register's Corey Quinn analysis frames Abbeel as positioned to leverage Amazon's deployed robotics fleet (warehouses) as a data and capability advantage for embodied AI versus OpenAI and Anthropic.

GDP / @bookwormengr X post (https://x.com/bookwormengr/status/2001353147055509881): *"Congrats Pieter Abbeel @pabbeel — Amazon's new AGI Head. TPOT needs no introduction to him. He is a pioneer in Robotics and DeepRL. Has 231K citations."*

## Strategic implications

1. Abbeel now sits at the intersection of frontier-LLM and frontier-robotics research at the only Big Tech that has both massive deployed robot fleets AND a top-3 cloud business.
2. The dual-mandate (LLM + robotics) means his public stances in 2026 will increasingly fold language modeling concerns (RLHF, alignment, scaling laws) into a robotics frame — and vice versa.
3. Covariant's RFM line is no longer the spearhead; Amazon Nova and the next robotic models share the same leader.
4. Constraint: Abbeel's public commentary on competitive labs (Physical Intelligence, Skild, Figure, Tesla Optimus) will likely become more guarded under Amazon corporate communications.
