# Thomas Wolf — Biography and Background

Source crawl date: 2026-05-28. Subject identifier confidence: 0.95.

## Identity

- Full name: Thomas Wolf
- Nationality: French
- Current role: Co-founder and Chief Science Officer (CSO), Hugging Face (since 2016)
- Public handles: `@Thom_Wolf` on X; `thomwolf` on Hugging Face Hub and GitHub; `thom-wolf` on LinkedIn; personal site `thomwolf.io`

## Education

- PhD in statistical and quantum physics (superconducting physics), École Polytechnique / Strasbourg lineage; published in Physical Review B and related journals (Google Scholar profile)
- Law degree from Sorbonne University (so he could later qualify as a European Patent Attorney)
- Public bio at WEF / London Tech Week / Crunchbase / Sequoia all confirm physics PhD followed by law degree, then patent attorney work

## Pre-Hugging Face career

- European Patent Attorney working across USA, France, Netherlands
- ~2015: consulting for deep-learning / ML startups; rediscovered statistical physics underpinnings of modern ML
- Self-taught ML through books and online courses while still in patent law

## Hugging Face founding (2016)

- Co-founded with Clément Delangue (CEO) and Julien Chaumond (CTO)
- Three French entrepreneurs; company HQ in Manhattan, NYC; large Paris office
- Original product: a chatbot app aimed at teenagers (pivoted hard toward ML infrastructure within ~18 months)
- Company name from the 🤗 emoji (U+1F917)
- ~250 employees as of 2025; private; 2022 revenue ~$15M; subsequent valuation has grown substantially

## What Thomas built / led inside Hugging Face

- The `transformers` Python library — original lead author on the EMNLP 2020 demo paper (arXiv 1910.03771). The library has crossed 100k+ GitHub stars; among the most-starred AI repos in history.
- The `datasets` library
- The `tokenizers` library (Rust-backed)
- The `accelerate` distributed training library
- BigScience workshop — initiator and senior chair; the largest research collaboration ever assembled in AI (1,000+ researchers); produced BLOOM (176B open-access multilingual LLM, July 2022)
- SmolLM family (SmolLM, SmolLM2, SmolLM3) — Hugging Face's own open-weight LLM series
- LeRobot — open-source robotics platform; the SO-100 ($100 robotic arm), Reachy Mini ($299), and large-scale dataset hub
- Open R1 — Hugging Face's fully open reproduction of DeepSeek-R1 (Jan 2025)
- The Ultra-Scale Playbook — a ~100-page, ~30,000-word free open guide to training LLMs on GPU clusters (Feb 2025), drawn from 4,000+ scaling experiments using up to 512 GPUs
- Pollen Robotics acquisition (April 2025) — added French humanoid hardware (Reachy 2) to Hugging Face

## Books

- "Natural Language Processing with Transformers" (O'Reilly, 2022) — with Lewis Tunstall and Leandro von Werra. The canonical hands-on book for the transformers library era.
- Co-author / contributor to "The Ultra-Scale Playbook" (Hugging Face, 2025)
