# Safe Superintelligence Inc. — mission and approach

Source: https://ssi.inc/
Fetched: 2026-05-27

## Core mission statement

"Building safe superintelligence (SSI) is the most important technical problem of our time."

## Company description

The organization describes itself as "the world's first straight-shot SSI lab, with one goal and one product: a safe superintelligence." Their strategy involves pursuing safety and capabilities development simultaneously, aiming to "advance capabilities as fast as possible while making sure our safety always remains ahead."

## Key operational principles

- Singular dedication to SSI eliminates distractions from management overhead and product cycles.
- The business model insulates "safety, security, and progress" from short-term commercial pressures.
- Based in Palo Alto with offices in Tel Aviv.
- Focused on recruiting elite engineers and researchers.

## Positioning

The lab characterizes its approach as solving the SSI challenge "through revolutionary engineering and scientific breakthroughs" and describes the opportunity as enabling people to do "your life's work and help solve the most important technical challenge of our age."

The page emphasizes alignment across team, investors, and business operations toward the singular SSI objective. Notably, the website is described in press coverage (April 2025 TechCrunch) as "little more than a placeholder with a mission statement."
