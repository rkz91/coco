## Capital raises

| Date | Amount | Valuation | Lead investor(s) |
|------|--------|-----------|------------------|
| September 2024 | $1B | ~$5B | SV Angel, DST Global, Sequoia Capital, Andreessen Horowitz |
| March 2025 | $2B | ~$30B | Greenoaks Capital (with a16z, Lightspeed, DST Global; Alphabet and Nvidia also backed) |
| April 2025 (reported) | $2B | ~$32B | Greenoaks led ($500M); valuation marked up further |

Total raised by April 2025: ~$3B.

## Strategic partnerships

- **April 2025**: Google Cloud announced TPU provision for SSI research.

## Acquisition activity

- **First half of 2025**: Meta attempted to acquire SSI. Offer was rejected by Sutskever.

## Leadership changes

- **June 29, 2025**: Daniel Gross's last day at SSI; departed to join Meta Superintelligence Labs.
- **July 3, 2025**: Ilya Sutskever assumed CEO role.
- **July 2025**: Daniel Levy assumed President role.

## Key quotes from Sutskever's July 2025 message to team and investors

(Source: TechCrunch reproduction of the X/Twitter post.)

> "As you know, Daniel Gross's time with us has been winding down, and as of June 29 he is officially no longer a part of SSI. We are grateful for his early contributions to the company and wish him well in his next [chapter]."

> "You might have heard rumors of companies looking to acquire us. We are flattered by their attention but are focused on seeing our work through."

> "We have the compute, we have the team, and we know what to do. Together we will keep building safe superintelligence."

## Notable financial-context items

- ~20 employees at the time of the $32B valuation (April 2025).
- No revenue. No product. No public technical disclosures.
- Valuation is a function of (a) Sutskever's brand, (b) the singular-mission thesis, and (c) the AI lab supply constraint.
