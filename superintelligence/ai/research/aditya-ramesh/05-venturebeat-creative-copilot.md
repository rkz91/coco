# VentureBeat — "Two years after DALL-E debut, its inventor is 'surprised' by impact"

- **URL:** https://venturebeat.com/ai/two-years-after-dall-e-debut-its-inventor-is-surprised-by-impact
- **Publication date:** January 2023 (two-year retrospective on the January 2021 DALL-E launch)
- **Author:** VentureBeat (Sharon Goldman)

## Direct Ramesh quotes captured in this profile and its companion homegrown.co.in piece

- **On motivation for text-to-image as a research target:** "We felt like text-to-image generation was interesting because as humans, we're able to construct a sentence to describe any situation we might encounter in real life, but also fantastical situations or crazy scenarios that are impossible."
- **On his framing of DALL·E for creative work:** he describes DALL·E as a "**creative co-pilot**" for artists, analogous to how Codex serves programmers.
- **On the speed of adoption:** "I knew that the technology was going to get to a point where it would be impactful to consumers and useful for many different applications, but I was still surprised by how quickly."
- **On deployment cadence:** Ramesh states that OpenAI is "being careful about deployment and making sure to have all bases covered before putting new generative AI tools in the hands of the general public." (Paraphrased from VentureBeat; appears in 2022-2023 coverage of DALL·E 2's research preview.)

## What this source establishes for the persona

1. **The "creative co-pilot" framing.** This is Ramesh's house metaphor for what generative-image models are *for*. It is the same framing Karpathy uses for code (LLMs as co-pilots for programmers) — both come out of the OpenAI culture of 2021–2023. This is his stated product philosophy.
2. **Text-to-image as a chosen problem.** "Construct a sentence to describe... fantastical situations or crazy scenarios that are impossible." Ramesh is pulled toward generative-image because **language is the most general specification interface for visual concepts**. This is the precursor of his Sora-era stance that "language grounding" is the unique edge of OpenAI's stack.
3. **Caution about deployment** — not a doomer stance, but explicit recognition that artist-adjacent generative media will be socially contested. Companion DALL-E 2 deployment material confirms OpenAI invited 3,000+ artists across 118 countries into early research preview before opening the floodgates.
4. **A persona who is "surprised" by adoption speed.** This is consistent with his low-volume public surface — he is not a hype maximalist. He builds and ships and lets the artifact do the talking. The contrast with Sam Altman's public-facing role is sharp and load-bearing for the persona's voice.
