## Public role timeline visible on the site and confirmed cross-source

- **January 2021** — DALL·E launch. Ramesh listed as first author of the OpenAI blog post and the ICML 2021 paper. Reportedly developed the approach, trained the models, and wrote most of the blog copy.
- **April 2022** — DALL·E 2 launch. Co-created with Prafulla Dhariwal, Alex Nichol, Casey Chu, Mark Chen. First-author on the unCLIP paper "Hierarchical Text-Conditional Image Generation with CLIP Latents" (arXiv 2204.06125).
- **September 2023** — DALL·E 3 ships, integrated directly into ChatGPT. Ramesh led the team. The accompanying paper "Improving Image Generation with Better Captions" is led by James Betker; Ramesh is named in the author list as the senior research figure.
- **February 2024** — Sora preview. Ramesh credited as Sora team lead alongside Tim Brooks and Bill Peebles.
- **December 2024** — Sora ships publicly via sora.com. Ramesh led the team that built the model and the consumer product surface.
- **September 30, 2025** — Sora 2 ships. Ramesh's team focused on physical realism, prompt fidelity, controllability, and language grounding.
- **As of late-2025 / 2026** — Promoted/transitioned to VP of Research, leading "Worldsim" (the World Simulation team). Bootstrapping an OpenAI robotics effort that takes the video-generation prior into physical actuation. Some social channels list his title as "VP Robotics at OpenAI"; the personal site says "VP of Research."
