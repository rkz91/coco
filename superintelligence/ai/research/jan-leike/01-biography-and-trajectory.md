## Education

- **Undergraduate:** University of Freiburg (Germany).
- **Master's:** Computer Science.
- **PhD:** Australian National University, machine learning / reinforcement learning theory, supervised by **Marcus Hutter**. Defended 2016. Dissertation built on a mathematical model for artificial general intelligence (Solomonoff / AIXI tradition inherited from Hutter).
- **Post-doc:** Future of Humanity Institute (Oxford), short stint before joining DeepMind.

## Career timeline

1. **DeepMind (≈2015 – 2021).** Alignment researcher on Shane Legg's safety team. Prototyped reinforcement learning from human feedback in collaboration with OpenAI (the canonical Christiano & Leike 2017 paper was a joint OpenAI/DeepMind effort). Co-authored the 2018 "Scalable agent alignment via reward modeling: a research direction" paper that proposed **recursive reward modeling** as a research agenda.
2. **OpenAI (2021 – May 2024).** Head of Alignment. Contributed to InstructGPT, ChatGPT post-training, and GPT-4 alignment. In **June 2023**, OpenAI announced the **Superalignment team** co-led by Leike and Ilya Sutskever, committing 20% of OpenAI's compute over four years to align superintelligence. TIME 100 in AI in both 2023 and 2024.
3. **Public resignation, May 17, 2024.** Posted a viral X thread the day after Sutskever's announced departure. Core line: *"Over the past years, safety culture and processes have taken a backseat to shiny products."* Said he had been "sailing against the wind," that the team was "under-resourced," and that he had "gradually lost trust" in OpenAI's leadership. OpenAI dissolved the Superalignment team within days.
4. **Anthropic (May 28, 2024 – present).** Announced on X: *"I'm excited to join @AnthropicAI to continue the superalignment mission! My new team will work on scalable oversight, weak-to-strong generalization, and automated alignment research."* Currently co-leads Anthropic's **Alignment Science** team.
5. **May 8, 2026.** Tweets: *"Some personal news: I am starting a new research project at Anthropic. Very excited about this! Many things are needed to make AGI go well, and alignment is only one of them."* (Status URL `x.com/janleike/status/2052807760291733505`.) Signals a focus shift within Anthropic; specifics not yet public as of last verification.
