# Nathan Lambert — recent signals research (post 2025-05-27)

All entries dated after May 27, 2025 to satisfy `recent_signal_12mo` requirement.

## Confirmed post-2025-05-27 items

### "Towards American Truly Open Models: The ATOM Project"
- **Date:** August 4, 2025 (Interconnects post); rebrand of his "American DeepSeek Project" from July 4, 2025.
- **URL:** https://www.interconnects.ai/p/atom-project
- **Takeaway:** Calls for America to build and maintain at least one lab training open models with 10,000+ leading-edge GPUs. Documents that Chinese open models (DeepSeek, Qwen) had taken over the top of LMArena and ArtificialAnalysis open-model leaderboards by August 2025. Frames the issue as urgent and tractable in 6–12 months, not years. The signature 2025 political/strategic position.

### RewardBench 2 release
- **Date:** June 2025 (arXiv:2506.01937).
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.01937
- **Takeaway:** Reward model evaluation continues to be a research bottleneck. Models score ~20 points lower on RB2 than RB1. Affirms his long-running thesis that reward models, not policies, are the limiting factor in RLHF quality.

### "RewardBench 2 and the state of preference finetuning"
- **Date:** mid-2025, Interconnects.
- **URL:** https://natolambert.substack.com/p/rewardbench-2-and-the-state-of-preference

### Olmo 3 release announcement
- **Date:** November 20, 2025.
- **URL:** https://www.interconnects.ai/p/olmo-3-americas-truly-open-reasoning
- **Takeaway:** First-ever fully open 32B thinking (reasoning-chain) model. Four 7B variants: Base, Instruct, Think, RL Zero. Plus 32B Think and 32B Base. OlmoTrace tool ties outputs to training data. Lambert calls the 32B base "our most impactful artifact." Direct quote on Olmo 3: "competitive on performance – not just replications of models that came out 6 to 12 months ago."

### "2025 Interconnects year in review"
- **Date:** December 2025.
- **URL:** https://www.interconnects.ai/p/2025-interconnects-year-in-review
