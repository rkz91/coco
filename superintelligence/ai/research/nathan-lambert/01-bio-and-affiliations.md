## Current role (2026)

- **Senior Research Scientist and post-training lead at the Allen Institute for AI (Ai2)**, leading open language-model post-training including the Olmo and Tülu families. Source: Interconnects "About" page; natolambert.com homepage; LinkedIn.
- Founder and primary author of the **Interconnects** newsletter and podcast (interconnects.ai). Publishes 1–3 times per week with 300+ posts as of early 2026.
- Founder of the **ATOM Project** (American Truly Open Models), launched July 4, 2025 (as "American DeepSeek Project") and rebranded in August 2025. atomproject.ai.
