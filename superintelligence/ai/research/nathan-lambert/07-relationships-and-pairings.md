- **John Schulman** — RL peer; Schulman has publicly endorsed Interconnects as "the best blog on this topic." Lambert covers Schulman's PPO lineage at length in the RLHF Book. Shared technical vocabulary.

## Productive conflict with

- **Dario Amodei** — closed-frontier-lab vs open-ecosystem. Amodei's policy frame is that frontier capabilities are too dangerous to ship openly until verified safe; Lambert's frame is that closed verification is a contradiction in terms and the open ecosystem is the verification mechanism. Both serious, both right about different things — the productive disagreement.
- **Sam Altman** — frontier-only commercial-lab posture vs broader-access open-models posture. ATOM Project is in many ways an explicit answer to the OpenAI strategy.
- **Yoshua Bengio** (potential) — safety-first pause-the-frontier stance vs open-ship-faster stance. Lambert respects the safety frame but pushes on its closed-lab implications.
- **Demis Hassabis** (potential) — DeepMind's closed-by-default science stance vs Ai2's open-by-default science stance.
