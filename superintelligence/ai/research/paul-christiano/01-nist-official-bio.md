# Paul Christiano — NIST Official Biography (extracted 2026-05-27)

Source: https://www.nist.gov/people/paul-christiano

## Identity

- **Full name:** Paul F. Christiano
- **Current title:** Head of AI Safety, U.S. Artificial Intelligence Safety Institute (housed at NIST; the institute was renamed Center for AI Standards and Innovation, CAISI, in 2025).
- **NIST contact:** paul.christiano [at] nist.gov; (240) 961-1973; Director's Office — HQ.

## Education (per NIST page)

- **B.S.** Mathematics, Massachusetts Institute of Technology
- **Ph.D.** Computer Science, University of California, Berkeley

## Role at AISI / CAISI

NIST describes his remit verbatim as: "designs and conducts tests of frontier AI models, with emphasis on model evaluations for capabilities of national security concern." He also "contributes guidance on evaluations and implementation of safety and security risk mitigations."

## Prior affiliations (per NIST page)

- **Founder, Alignment Research Center (ARC)** — a nonprofit focused on aligning machine learning systems with human interests through theoretical research.
- **Founder, third-party frontier-model evaluation initiative** that was spun off into **Model Evaluation and Threat Research (METR)**.
- **OpenAI** — directed the language model alignment team; NIST credits him as a pioneer of "reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF), a foundational technical AI safety technique."
