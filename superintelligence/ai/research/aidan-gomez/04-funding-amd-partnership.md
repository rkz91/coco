# Source 04 — TechCrunch: Cohere $7B valuation + AMD partnership

Fetched: 2026-05-27
URL: https://techcrunch.com/2025/09/24/cohere-hits-7b-valuation-a-month-after-its-last-raise-partners-with-amd/

## Headline facts

- **Date:** September 24, 2025
- **Valuation:** $7 billion (via $100M extension to August round)
- **Prior round:** $500M at $6.8B (August 2025)
- **Total fresh capital (Aug+Sept 2025):** $600M
- **New investors:** Business Development Bank of Canada (BDC), Nexxus Capital Management (Singapore)

## AMD partnership

- Cohere's Command-family AI models (vision, translate, reasoning variants) are now compatible with AMD Instinct GPU.
- AMD will use Cohere internally as a customer (reciprocal partnership).
- Cohere maintains support for Nvidia GPUs alongside AMD.

## Strategic positioning quote (paraphrased)

- The company targets enterprises prioritizing "AI sovereignty" — maintaining local control of data and models rather than external dependencies on hyperscalers or US labs.

## Founder reference

- "Aidan Gomez co-founded the company in 2019 and authored the 'transformer' paper enabling modern generative AI advancement."

## Notes for synthesis

- AI sovereignty is the framing Gomez has leaned into through 2025-2026 — Canada, UK, Korea, Japan, Germany (Aleph Alpha acquisition), Spain (The Logic 2026 article on Madrid-Ottawa AI collab). This is the "enterprise-first, non-US-hyperscaler" pitch globalized.
- Hardware diversity (Nvidia + AMD) is the same independence-from-bottleneck story: Cohere wants no single vendor lock.
