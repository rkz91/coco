## Company snapshot

- **Founded:** 2019 by Aidan Gomez, Ivan Zhang, and Nick Frosst (all University of Toronto alumni)
- **Headquarters:** Toronto, Ontario, Canada
- **Offices:** Montreal, New York City, San Francisco, London, Paris, Seoul
- **Employees:** 450+ (2025); ~842 per other sources by Feb 2026
- **Focus:** Large language models and AI for regulated industries (finance, healthcare, manufacturing, energy, public sector)

## Products (2026)

- **Command A+** — latest flagship; 218B parameters; Apache 2.0; native citation grounding; runs on single B200 or 2x H100
- **Command A Reasoning / A Translate / A Vision** — domain variants of the A line
- **Command R, Command R+** — 2024 generation (still widely deployed)
- **North** — secure enterprise agent workspace platform (launched January 2025)
- **Aya / Aya Vision / Tiny Aya** — open-source multilingual research models
- **Embed v3, Rerank 3** — retrieval-first product line
- **Transcribe** (March 2026) — open-source speech-to-text, 2B params, 14 languages
- **Compass** — multimodal search/discovery framework underlying North
