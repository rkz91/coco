## Cohere Labs (formerly Cohere For AI)

- Launched June 2022 as a nonprofit research lab inside Cohere.
- Originally led by Sara Hooker (ex-Google Brain).
- Sara Hooker departed September 2025 to co-found Adaption Labs with Sudip Roy ($50M seed, Feb 2026, led by Emergence Capital).
- Now led by Marzieh Fadaee.

## The Aya initiative

- Aya is a state-of-the-art, open-source, massively multilingual research LLM covering 101 languages — including more than 50 previously underserved languages.
- Built collaboratively with **over 3,000 researchers from 119 countries**.
- Distribution: open weights, free for research use.

## Aya timeline

- **2024:** Aya 23 / Aya Expanse open-weight family release.
- **March 2025:** Aya Vision — multimodal model that can describe images, translate text, summarize.
- **February 2026:** Tiny Aya — open-weight 3.35B-parameter models supporting 70+ languages; designed to run locally on laptops and edge devices without internet connectivity.

## Why this matters for Gomez's persona

- The Aya program is the **research-credibility flank** of Cohere's enterprise-first commercial line.
- It anchors the "AI must work in every language, not just English" claim with peer-reviewed work and a global research community.
- It is also the structural answer to the persistent critique that Cohere doesn't publish as much research as DeepMind / OpenAI / Anthropic — Aya is the published-research output.
- Hooker's departure (Sept 2025) is a strategic question mark — Cohere Labs continued shipping (Aya Vision, Tiny Aya) under Fadaee, but the public face of the research community has changed.
