## Persona-relevant framings

- **World models as the next frontier.** Hassabis spent most of the podcast on this thread. Video generation is incidental; the underlying claim is that AGI requires a model of physical reality that current LLMs lack.
- **Conjecture > solution.** This is the deepest novel framing in the episode. Most public AGI discourse focuses on benchmark performance (solving). Hassabis explicitly inverts it: the harder, less-discussed capability is *coming up with the right question*.
- **AGI is a science project.** Repeatedly distinguishes engineering scale-ups from scientific breakthroughs ("one or two more breakthroughs"). This is the consistent counter to the pure-scaling-hypothesis camp.
