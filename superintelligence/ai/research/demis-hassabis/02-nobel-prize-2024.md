# Nobel Prize in Chemistry 2024 — Hassabis and Jumper

Source: https://deepmind.google/blog/demis-hassabis-john-jumper-awarded-nobel-prize-in-chemistry/
Cross-reference: https://www.nobelprize.org/prizes/chemistry/2024/press-release/, https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/09/deepminds-demis-hassabis-and-john-jumper-scoop-nobel-prize-in-chemistry-for-alphafold/

## Citation

- Awarded 9 October 2024 by the Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences.
- One half jointly to **Demis Hassabis** and **John M. Jumper** "for protein structure prediction."
- The other half to **David Baker** (University of Washington) "for computational protein design."
- The prize specifically recognised **AlphaFold 2** (published Nature 2021), one of the most-cited scientific publications of the past five years.

## Impact statement (as cited)

- AlphaFold Protein Structure Database hosts more than 200 million predicted structures.
- Over **2 million researchers** from **190 countries** have accessed predictions via the public database.
- Use cases publicly cited: enzyme design, drug discovery, malaria vaccine work, antibiotic-resistance research.

## Hassabis quote

> "AlphaFold has already been used by more than two million researchers to advance critical work, from enzyme design to drug discovery." — Demis Hassabis, on the day of the Nobel announcement.

## Jumper quote

> "AlphaFold delivered on this promise" — Jumper, framing AlphaFold as the demonstration that computational biology could provide practical insights for experimentalists and disease researchers.
