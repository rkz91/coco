## What the Scholar profile signals

1. The portfolio is consistently and clearly retrieval-themed. Even the 2024 papers are retrieval- or evaluation-adjacent — KV cache for retrieval-heavy contexts, LLM-judge panels for RAG eval reliability.
2. Lewis publishes less under his own first-author byline post-2022. The work is now under his direction (Cohere modelling team) and shows up either as institutional papers (Command A technical report) or as co-authorships on collaborator-led projects.
3. The 39K-citation total without a frontier-model or AGI-themed paper underlines his thesis: retrieval is its own load-bearing research line, not a side-quest off pretraining.
