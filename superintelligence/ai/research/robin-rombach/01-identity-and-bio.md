# Robin Rombach — Identity and Bio

## Identification (high confidence)

- **Full name:** Robin Rombach
- **Born:** 1994, Germany
- **Nationality:** German
- **Current role (May 2026):** Co-founder and CEO of Black Forest Labs (founded August 2024). Based in Freiburg im Breisgau, Germany.
- **X / Twitter handle:** [@robrombach](https://twitter.com/robrombach)
- **GitHub handle:** [rromb](https://github.com/rromb)
- **Google Scholar:** [author profile](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=ygdQhrIAAAAJ&hl=en)
- **Confidence:** 0.95. Identity is unambiguous; every public profile listed above resolves to the same person — lead author of the CVPR 2022 latent diffusion paper, founding researcher of Stable Diffusion, and named CEO of BFL in every funding announcement.

## Education

- **2013–2020:** Physics, University of Heidelberg.
- **PhD:** Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München (LMU Munich), advised by Björn Ommer (Computer Vision and Learning Group, formerly at Heidelberg). PhD work covered VQGAN, Taming Transformers, and Latent Diffusion Models.
- **Thesis line of work:** efficient generative models — compressing perceptual content into a latent space before running iterative generative dynamics.

## Past affiliations

- **Stability AI** (2022–March 2024). Joined as a research scientist; rose to Research Director on the scientific/research team. Lead researcher behind Stable Diffusion 1/2/XL/3. Departed publicly in March 2024 along with Andreas Blattmann, Dominik Lorenz, and several other Stable Diffusion authors. Reasons not officially disclosed; Stability AI was simultaneously in financial difficulty and had cycled through three rounds of senior departures.
- **Ommer Lab, LMU Munich** (PhD years through 2022). Co-author on the foundational latent diffusion paper.

## Co-founders at Black Forest Labs

The original Stable Diffusion team carried over largely intact:

- **Patrick Esser** — co-author of LDM paper, SD3, FLUX. Co-founder.
- **Andreas Blattmann** — co-author of LDM paper, SD3, FLUX. Co-founder.
- **Dominik Lorenz** — co-author of LDM paper, SD3, FLUX. Co-founder.
- Additional ex-Stability researchers reported on the founding team: Müller, Kulal, Dockhorn, Sauer (per Sifted reporting). Approximately 10 cofounders/early hires total, ~5 of them ex-Stability.

## Personal style (per public coverage)

- Described by Sifted (December 2024) as "almost omnipotent" within the AI community for his technical depth; "prefers low profile"; satisfaction is "being acclaimed by the AI community" rather than press attention.
- Quoted as preferring to "let the product speak for itself" (Financial Times, via Sifted summary).
- "He's just a guy that loves to create and build AI" — characterization from a former colleague (Sifted).
