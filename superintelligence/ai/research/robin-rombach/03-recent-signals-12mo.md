# Robin Rombach — Recent Signals (last 12 months, post 2025-05-27)

Filter: published or announced between 2025-05-27 and 2026-05-27. Each entry includes a dated URL.

## 1. FLUX.1 Kontext launch — 2025-05-29

- **Source:** [Business Wire press release](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20250529605562/en/Black-Forest-Labs-Launches-FLUX.1-Kontext-a-Breakthrough-in-Context-aware-Image-Generation-and-Editing)
- **Takeaway:** First model in the BFL lineup that unifies image generation and image editing in a single flow-matching architecture. Rombach framed it as "a fundamental shift from traditional editing approaches." Up to 8× faster than competing edit-capable models at launch. Adobe integrated Kontext into Photoshop beta in September 2025.

## 2. Meta multi-year licensing deal — September 2025

- **Source:** [Tech.news.am summary](https://tech.news.am/eng/news/6086/meta-concludes-a-$140-million-deal-with-german-ai-startup-black-forest-labs.html) (cross-referenced via Sifted, Fortune)
- **Takeaway:** Reported $140M multi-year contract with Meta for BFL's generative image technology. Underwrites Meta's Vibes video-generation app and downstream visual-AI features. Validates the dual revenue model (open weights for ecosystem reach, enterprise licensing for revenue).

## 3. FLUX.2 [dev] release — 2025-11-25

- **Source:** [GitHub: black-forest-labs/flux2](https://github.com/black-forest-labs/flux2) and [Cloudflare Workers AI blog](https://blog.cloudflare.com/flux-2-workers-ai/)
- **Takeaway:** 32B-parameter open-weights model. Unifies text-to-image generation and image editing in one checkpoint. Pairs latent flow matching with Mistral-3 VLM for multi-image reference understanding. JSON prompting and hex-color control for enterprise predictability. "Physical-world understanding" framing — hands, fabrics, logos, lighting — directly targets the failure modes that have made image gen feel uncanny.

## 4. Series B raise — $300M at $3.25B — 2025-12-01

- **Source:** [TechCrunch coverage](https://techcrunch.com/2025/12/01/black-forest-labs-raises-300m-at-3-25b-valuation/) and [GlobeNewswire release](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2025/12/01/3196629/0/en/Black-Forest-Labs-Announces-Series-B-Investment-to-Accelerate-Frontier-Visual-Intelligence.html)
- **Co-leads:** Salesforce Ventures and Anjney Midha (AMP).
- **Participants:** a16z, NVIDIA, General Catalyst, Temasek, Bain Capital Ventures, Air Street Capital, Visionaries Club, Canva, Figma Ventures.
- **Rombach direct quotes from the announcement:**
  - "Visual AI is shifting from impressive image generation to genuine understanding and the market for our products is growing rapidly as a result."
  - "We're building multimodal models that unify perception, generation, and reasoning — foundational infrastructure for how we'll shape and experience the visual world."
- **Takeaway:** Total funding now $450M+. Confirms the shift from "image-gen specialist" to "multimodal visual intelligence" framing. Salesforce Ventures called BFL "foundational infrastructure for production-grade generative images."
