Total funds raised: $450M+ by end of 2025. Largest German AI raise to date. Became Germany's most-valuable AI company.

## Commercial integrations (enterprise partners)

- **xAI / Grok (August 2024):** FLUX.1 powered Grok-2 image generation. Later replaced by xAI's in-house Aurora model in December 2024.
- **Mistral AI / Le Chat (November 18, 2024):** Le Chat integrated FLUX Pro. Later upgraded to FLUX Ultra. Co-developed deal — 2K resolution in under 4 seconds.
- **NVIDIA (January 2025):** Strategic partnership; NVIDIA participated in Series A. FLUX.2 models RTX-optimized at launch.
- **Adobe (September 2025):** FLUX.1 Kontext Pro integrated into Photoshop beta.
- **Meta (September 2025):** Reported multi-year licensing contract worth ~$140M. BFL technology underpins Meta's Vibes video-generation app.
- **Cloudflare (November 25, 2025):** FLUX.2 [dev] launched on Workers AI same-day as the BFL release.
- **Salesforce Ventures (co-lead investor, December 2025):** Strategic distribution implied alongside the capital commitment.
- **Canva, Figma Ventures (Series B participants):** Strategic alignment for design-tool integration.
- **Microsoft (per Salesforce Ventures memo and Fortune coverage):** Among the enterprise partners using FLUX in production. Specific deal terms not disclosed.

## Revenue model (per Fortune, February 2026)

- Open-weights / open-source models build market reach and developer mindshare. No direct revenue.
- Commercial licensing (Adobe, Canva, Meta, Microsoft) — roughly half of revenue.
- Low-latency hosted API (BFL Playground, BFL API) — roughly the other half of revenue.
- Cited by Salesforce Ventures: 400M+ model downloads. The most-downloaded open-weights image model family in the world by late 2025.

## Strategic positioning quotes from investors

- **Salesforce Ventures:** BFL is "the foundational infrastructure for production-grade generative images." The bet is on the inflection point where generative media becomes "an essential, non-negotiable component of creative workflows."
- **Northzone (Series A participant):** "Into the new era of visual intelligence."
