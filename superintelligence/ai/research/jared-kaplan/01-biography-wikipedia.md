# Jared Kaplan — Biographical Synthesis

Sources: Wikipedia, Grokipedia, JHU Physics & Astronomy faculty directory, Hertz Foundation profile.

## Identity confirmation

- Real name: Jared Kaplan.
- Date confirmed: 2026-05-27 via Wikipedia, Johns Hopkins University Department of Physics & Astronomy faculty directory, and Anthropic public materials. High confidence.

## Education

- BSc in Physics and Mathematics — Stanford University.
- PhD in Physics — Harvard University, conferred 2009.
- Doctoral dissertation: "Aspects of holography," advised by Nima Arkani-Hamed.
- Hertz Fellow, 2005 cohort.

## Academic physics career

- Postdoctoral fellow, SLAC and Stanford University.
- Faculty appointment at Johns Hopkins University Department of Physics and Astronomy from 2012.
- Title progressed assistant -> associate professor. Reported across sources as currently on leave of absence while working at Anthropic.
- Research interests as cited on the JHU directory: effective field theory, particle physics, cosmology, scattering amplitudes, and conformal field theory.
- Present academic research uses conformal field theory to learn about quantum gravity via the AdS/CFT correspondence, leveraging the conformal-bootstrap programme.
- Awards in academia: Sloan Research Fellowship, NSF CAREER grant (PHY-1454083, "Conformal Field Theory and Quantum Gravity from the Bottom-Up"), affiliated with the Simons Collaboration on the Nonperturbative Bootstrap.

## OpenAI (2019 -> 2021)

- Joined OpenAI in 2019 as a researcher.
- First-author on "Scaling Laws for Neural Language Models" (arXiv 2001.08361, January 2020).
- Co-author on "Language Models are Few-Shot Learners" (arXiv 2005.14165, May 2020), the GPT-3 paper.
- Involved in early Codex / code-model development.

## Anthropic (May 2021 -> present)

- Co-founded Anthropic in 2021 alongside Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, Tom Brown, Jack Clark, Sam McCandlish, Chris Olah, and Ben Mann. Reporting consistently lists seven core co-founders with Kaplan among them.
- Title: Co-founder and Chief Science Officer.
- October 2024: Appointed Anthropic's Responsible Scaling Officer, succeeding Sam McCandlish in that role, with authority to determine the safety assessments and precautions required before model release.
- May 2025: Decided that Claude Opus 4 / Claude 4 series required release under AI Safety Level 3 (ASL-3) protections after internal tests indicated the model might assist amateur scientists in developing biological weapons.
- 2025-2026: Continued in this dual scientific and safety-governance role through the Claude 4 -> Sonnet 4.5 -> Opus 4.6 -> Sonnet 4.6 -> Opus 4.7 sequence.

## Personal

- Residence: Pacifica, California.
- Reported estimated net worth: $3.7 billion (Forbes, 2026).
- Co-signatory of the seven-co-founder pledge to commit 80% of personal fortunes to combat AI-driven inequality (2026).
