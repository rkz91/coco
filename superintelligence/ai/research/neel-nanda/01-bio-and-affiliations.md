# Neel Nanda — bio, affiliations, education

Source: https://www.neelnanda.io/about (fetched 2026-05-27)

## Current role

Neel Nanda runs the **Google DeepMind mechanistic interpretability team**. The team's stated work is to "reverse engineer the algorithms and structures" learned by neural networks during training. He has held this leadership role since the previous team lead stepped down; he initially joined DeepMind as an individual contributor without prior management experience.

## Mission framing

Neel describes the goal of his work as **"reducing existential risk from AI"** and identifies with the Effective Altruism and rationality communities. This frames how he prioritizes research directions — mech interp is a means, not an end.

## Education

- **University of Cambridge** — Pure Mathematics undergraduate, graduated 2020. Cambridge intellectual culture shows up in his writing (precise, opinionated, willing to take strong positions).

## Career path (chronological)

1. **Quantitative finance internships** — Jane Street and Jump Trading
