# Recent signals — past 12 months (May 2025 - May 2026)

All entries dated after 2025-05-27 unless noted. Used as `recent_signal_12mo` source for the persona profile.

## 1. "The Misguided Quest for Mechanistic AI Interpretability"

- Date: 2025-05-15 (just inside the 12-month cutoff for the persona).
- Venue: AI Frontiers blog (https://ai-frontiers.org/articles/the-misguided-quest-for-mechanistic-ai-interpretability)
- Takeaway: public declaration that mechanistic interpretability has failed to deliver value despite a decade of investment, and that representation engineering is the productive successor. Explicit challenge to Anthropic's interpretability program.

## 5. CAIS AI Wellbeing research

- Date: May 2026
- Venue: AI Safety Newsletter #72 (https://newsletter.safe.ai/p/aisn-72-new-research-on-ai-wellbeing)
- Title: "AI Wellbeing: Measuring and Improving the Functional Pleasure and Pain of AIs"
- Takeaway: substantive expansion of Hendrycks' scope. CAIS argues LLMs behave robustly as though they have functional wellbeing, even if not conscious. Opens AI welfare as a distinct safety subfield. Notable because it moves Hendrycks beyond the "AI as adversarial agent" framing into "AI as moral patient" territory, which is a meaningful evolution of his public stance.
