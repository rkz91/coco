# Liang Wenfeng — Premier Li Qiang (Jan 2025) and President Xi Jinping (Feb 2025) symposiums

## Premier Li Qiang symposium — January 20, 2025

Sources:
- https://www.scmp.com/tech/policy/article/3295662/beijing-meeting-puts-spotlight-chinas-new-face-ai-deepseek-founder-liang-wenfeng
- https://news.cgtn.com/news/2025-01-27/Behind-China-s-rising-AI-startup-DeepSeek-Who-is-Liang-Wenfeng--1AvkPM0cqXK/p.html

Retrieved: 2026-05-28

- **Date:** January 20, 2025 (same day as DeepSeek-R1's release).
- **Event:** Symposium with Experts, Entrepreneurs and Representatives from the Fields of Education, Science, Culture, Health and Sports, hosted by Chinese Premier **Li Qiang** in Beijing.
- **Liang's role:** One of **nine** invited industry experts. Asked to provide opinions and suggestions on a draft of the 2024 government work report.
- **Premier Li Qiang's framing:** Focused on "new growth drivers" created through technological innovation. Positioned DeepSeek's achievements as part of broader economic strategy at a moment of "significant disruption in global AI development."
- **Why this matters:** This is the first high-level political signal that Liang specifically — not just DeepSeek as a company — was being treated as a national strategic asset. SCMP described the meeting as putting Liang in "the spotlight" as "China's new face of AI."
- **Other 8 attendees:** SCMP did not name them; the closed-door format means the full list is not publicly available.

## President Xi Jinping symposium — February 17, 2025

Sources:
- https://www.cnbc.com/2025/02/17/chinas-xi-jinping-speaks-to-entrepreneurs-in-a-rare-high-profile-meeting-.html
- https://pamirllc.com/blog/beijing-holds-private-enterprise-symposium-signaling-support-for-the-chinese-private-sector
- https://www.fredgao.com/p/xi-held-symposium-on-private-enterprises

Retrieved: 2026-05-28

- **Date:** February 17, 2025.
- **Event:** Private enterprise symposium at the Great Hall of the People.
- **Front-row attendees included:**
  - Zeng Yuqun (founder of CATL)
  - Jack Ma (founder of Alibaba)
  - Pony Ma (Tencent)
  - Liang Wenfeng (DeepSeek)
- **Significance:** 4th such symposium since 2018; 2nd attended by Xi personally (he skipped 2020 and 2024). Coming weeks after Trump's second-term tariffs on Chinese exports, the symposium was widely read as the CCP signaling renewed support for private enterprise after years of regulatory crackdown.
- **Xi quote:** "It is time for private enterprises and private entrepreneurs to show their talents."
- **This was Liang's last confirmed public appearance.** Per SCMP April 28, 2026 reporting, he has not made public appearances since.

## Pattern across both symposiums

- Liang was elevated from a quant-fund founder to a strategically important national figure in less than 30 days (Jan 20 → Feb 17, 2025), bracketing R1's release and the Nvidia market crash.
- He is now the *only* Chinese AI founder to sit at this level of political proximity. Robin Li (Baidu), Wang Xiaochuan (Baichuan), and other Chinese AI CEOs were not invited to the Xi symposium.
- The combination — international media (TIME, Fortune) + top-level Chinese political (Li Qiang, Xi) recognition — is what anchors his "lead-driver" status in the frontier-labs-research cell.
