# TIME 100 Most Influential People in AI 2025 — Liang Wenfeng

Source: https://time.com/collections/time100-ai-2025/7305843/liang-wenfeng-ai/

## Listing details

- **Category:** Leaders (alongside Ren Zhengfei of Huawei, Elon Musk of xAI, Sam Altman of OpenAI).
- TIME's framing: "soon markets were reeling, US tech dominance was being openly questioned, and Liang had been propelled to global renown."

## TIME's framing of his impact

- On January 20, 2025, DeepSeek released R1 — "the first open-weight model that challenged rival OpenAI's then-newest release."
- DeepSeek matched OpenAI performance "while using just a fraction of the bleeding-edge chips produced by Nvidia."
- Nvidia "lost $600 billion in market value, the largest drop for a single company in U.S. stock-market history."
- By February, DeepSeek had surpassed ChatGPT as the No. 1 free app on Apple's US App Store.

## TIME's biographical framing

- Grew up in "a tiny village in China's southern province of Guangdong."
- Studied computer science at Zhejiang University. (Note: Wikipedia says electronic information / information & communication engineering — TIME's "computer science" is a popular simplification.)
- May 2023: announced High-Flyer would pursue AGI; launched DeepSeek.
