# Thinking Machines Lab tenure — full timeline

Sources: Wikipedia article on Thinking Machines Lab; Implicator article "Thinking Machines Ousts CTO Zoph; Three Return to OpenAI"; Neowin coverage; HPCWire AIwire "The Thinking Machines Lab Saga"; Mira Murati X announcement; Fidji Simo OpenAI announcement.

## Founding (February 2025)

Thinking Machines Lab was launched in February 2025 by **Mira Murati** with approximately 30 hires, most from OpenAI:

- Mira Murati (CEO)
- **Barret Zoph** (co-founder, Chief Technology Officer)
- John Schulman (co-founder, Chief Scientist) — OpenAI co-founder and Zoph's long-time collaborator
- Lilian Weng (co-founder) — former OpenAI VP of Research, safety
- Andrew Tulloch (co-founder) — ex-Meta, departed October 2025
- Luke Metz (co-founder) — ex-OpenAI, departed January 2026 with Zoph

July 2025 seed round: **$2B at $12B valuation** (Lightspeed-led). One of the largest seeds in tech history.

## Public product releases under Zoph as CTO

### Tinker (October 1, 2025)
- **Managed fine-tuning API** for open-weight language models.
- Lets developers submit fine-tuning jobs through the API without managing distributed training infrastructure.
- Supports SFT, RL, and low-level primitives.
- Generally available December 12, 2025.
- The hint asks "was he on byline?" — Tinker was released as a Thinking Machines product, attributed institutionally to the lab; Zoph as CTO was clearly responsible for engineering direction but not personally credited as an author on the launch blog.

### Interaction Models (May 11, 2026)
- **Native multimodal real-time architecture.** 200ms micro-turn design; encoder-free early fusion (dMel audio, 40x40 image patches).
- Allows simultaneous input/output streams; dual-system design with a fast interaction model plus a background reasoning model.
- State-of-the-art on Audio MultiChallenge and FD-bench.
- **Released four months AFTER Zoph's departure**, so attributable to Soumith Chintala / John Schulman / the post-Zoph team. Zoph was not credited.
- URL: https://thinkingmachines.ai/blog/interaction-models/

## The firing (January 14, 2026)

Per Implicator and Wired (Kylie Robison reporting):

- **Monday (Jan 12, 2026):** Zoph informs Murati he is considering leaving.
- **Thursday (Jan 14, 2026):** Murati fires him at an all-hands meeting. Stated reason: "unethical conduct" — specifically "sharing confidential company information with competitors." (Wired could not verify this claim independently.)
- **Thursday (hours later):** OpenAI announces Zoph's return. Fidji Simo's statement specifically contradicts the Murati characterization, saying the move "has been in the works for several weeks" and OpenAI "doesn't share Murati's concerns about Zoph."
- Luke Metz and Sam Schoenholz return with Zoph; both report to him.
- Soumith Chintala named new Thinking Machines CTO.

## Aftermath / implications

- **John Schulman is the only original founder besides Murati remaining at TML.**
- Andrew Tulloch left to Meta in October 2025.
- Only ~10 of the original ~30 founding researchers remained by early 2026 according to AIwire.
- Zoph's return to OpenAI puts him under Fidji Simo (Applications division), not under the research org — meaning his focus is now on applied products and post-training of shipped models, not pure research.
