## Arthur Mensch's quote on the ASML round (Sept 9, 2025)

> "This investment brings together two technology leaders operating in the same value chain. We have the ambition to help ASML and its numerous partners solve current and future engineering challenges through AI, and ultimately to advance the full semiconductor and AI value chain."

The framing matters: ASML is the EU's most strategic technology asset (sole producer of EUV lithography). Mensch positioned Mistral as the AI complement to that hardware moat — explicitly a European semiconductor-plus-AI value chain.

ASML CFO Roger Dassen took a seat on Mistral's strategic committee board.

## Key partnerships

### Microsoft (February 2024)
Mistral Large made available on Azure. $16M strategic investment alongside. Mensch's framing: multi-cloud strategy reduces vendor lock-in for European enterprise customers.

### NVIDIA — Nemotron Coalition (announced March 16, 2026)
Source: https://mistral.ai/news/mistral-ai-and-nvidia-partner-to-accelerate-open-frontier-models ; https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/nvidia-launches-nemotron-coalition-of-leading-global-ai-labs-to-advance-open-frontier-models

- Mistral is a **founding member** of the NVIDIA Nemotron Coalition alongside Black Forest Labs, Cursor, LangChain, Perplexity, Reflection AI, Sarvam, and Thinking Machines Lab.
- Initial focus: open base model trained on NVIDIA DGX Cloud, underpinning the Nemotron 4 family.
- Mistral 3 family (announced same time) is jointly optimized across NVIDIA supercomputing (GB200 NVL72) and edge platforms (Spark, RTX, Jetson).
- Arthur Mensch quote: *"Open frontier models are how AI becomes a true platform. Together with NVIDIA, we will take a leading role in training and advancing frontier models at scale."*

## Datacenter / compute capacity

- Target: 200 MW of European compute capacity by 2027.
- Locations: France (near Paris) + Sweden.
- March 2026: $830M debt facility raised for build-out.
- May 2026: Mensch told CNBC Mistral has invested €4B into datacenters in France and Sweden.

## Headcount and revenue

- ~1,000 employees as of 2026 (up from ~350 in 2025).
- €1B revenue target for end-2026 (per National Assembly testimony).
- €1B R&D spend per year (per National Assembly testimony).
- 75% European sales; <30% foreign capital ownership.
