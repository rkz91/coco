## Pairs well with — who amplifies her

- **barret-zoph** — OpenAI post-training peer. Same surface, complementary registers (Barret on algorithms / infra, Karina on data + product).
- **john-schulman** — RLHF foundational practitioner. Karina extends his framing into the product layer. Schulman's Tinker / interaction-model work in 2025 explicitly takes the co-design loop seriously.
- **nathan-lambert** — Open RLHF / Tülu / Olmo lead at Ai2. Different camps (closed-lab post-training vs open post-training) but the same fundamental belief that *the post-training recipe matters more than the architecture*. They sharpen each other on data-mixture taste.
- **jan-leike** — Post-training + alignment crossover at Anthropic. Her capability-as-alignment stance lines up with the kind of question Leike works on.

## Productive conflict with — who sharpens her by disagreeing

- **dario-amodei** — Anthropic CEO. He represents the more closed, more safety-cautious post-training school she left. Their conflict surface is "how much of post-training research belongs in public writing." She publishes essays; he closes the lab. Both are right inside their own frames.
- **ilya-sutskever** — Scaling-first, pretraining-first worldview. Karina's position is that post-training is where the next round of progress lives and that capability is bottlenecked by human creativity, not by FLOPs. They disagree on where the marginal hour of research should go.
- **yann-lecun** — RLHF skeptic; sees RL on human feedback as a fundamentally limited paradigm. Karina's "RL is shaping up to be the most consequential phase" is the position LeCun has spent two years attacking. They sharpen each other.

## Why these specific personas

- Schulman, Zoph, Lambert, Leike all spend time defending the same broad claim (post-training matters; the recipe matters; the algorithm is downstream of the data) from different angles. Convening them together produces a converged synthesis on data-mixture practice.
- Amodei, Sutskever, LeCun each disagree with her at a different layer (institutional, scaling, paradigmatic). Convening her with one of them produces a sharper position than convening her alone.
