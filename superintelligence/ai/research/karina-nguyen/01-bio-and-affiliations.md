## Identification note

The user-supplied HINTS section claimed Brown University for her undergraduate and "data journalism / interactive graphics" at the New York Times. Independent web search contradicts both points. Her personal website, the Berkeley Nuclear Policy Working Group profile page, and multiple LinkedIn references uniformly cite **UC Berkeley** as her undergraduate institution (data science, sustainable design, public policy; previously architecture). Her NYT work was at the **internal R&D / design-systems team on the editor tool "Oak"** during 2020–2021 — i.e. software engineering for the newsroom's CMS, not bylined interactive journalism. This profile reflects the verified record, not the hint.

## Current role (2026)

- **Research Manager and Post-Training Lead in Model Behavior, OpenAI** — joined May 2024. Builds new interaction paradigms for reasoning interfaces and capabilities. Credited contributions over her first ~1.3 years at OpenAI include ChatGPT Canvas (described as "the first full synthetic post-training"), ChatGPT Tasks, SimpleQA, the 4o November 2024 release with improved writing, and the o-series reasoning models (o1, o3-with-tools). Source: karinanguyen.com, Latent Space podcast, Stanford NLP seminar bio, Andrew Ng's LinkedIn post.

## Past affiliations

- **Anthropic** (2022–2024, May): joined as employee ~#60 in the role of "first designer / front-end engineer," left as a researcher when the company exceeded 500–700 people. Led post-training and evaluations for Claude 2 and Claude 3 (Opus, Sonnet, Haiku), built the original claude.ai chat interface, the 100K-context document upload feature, and Claude Instant 1.2. Contributed to Constitutional AI scaling-law research, model personality / character work, hallucination mitigation, honesty, refusals, self-correction, and faithful reasoning. Source: x.com/karinanguyen_/status/1792996299069071760 (May 21, 2024 transition tweet); karinanguyen.com.
- **New York Times** (2020–2021): R&D / design systems on the "Oak" editor and the internal Application Catalog developer tool. Source: karinanguyen.com/projects.
- **Square** (2020): Virtual Terminal and Batch Invoices product design.
- **Dropbox** (2019): Notifications management; Chefs Budgeting Tool.
- **Primer.ai** (early career, listed on karinanguyen.com/about): R&D prototypes.
- **SITU Research / Bellingcat / Amnesty International / Associated Press / Bloomberg / Wired / CNN / Atlantic Council** (2019–2022): contributor on visual-forensics human-rights investigations (Ukraine cultural-site destruction; Russia–Ukraine cyber war crimes; Myanmar coup documentation; Beit Hanoun strike timeline; Capitol Riot rapid response; Portland federal-arrest investigation; Lebanese / Israeli police use-of-force documentation). Her contributions to the Russia–Ukraine war crimes work fed into Pulitzer-recognized reporting.
