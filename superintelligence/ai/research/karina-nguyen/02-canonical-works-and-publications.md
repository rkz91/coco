# Karina Nguyen — canonical works and publications

Pulled 2026-05-27. Anchor list for `canonical_works` and `key_publications` frontmatter fields.

## Co-authored papers (Google Scholar, top citations as of mid-2026)

1. **OpenAI o1 System Card** — Jaech, Kalai, Lerer, Richardson, Nguyen, et al. arXiv 2412.16720 (December 2024). ~2,150 citations. Foundational system card for the o1 reasoning model family.
2. **Towards Monosemanticity: Decomposing Language Models with Dictionary Learning** — Bricken, Templeton, Batson, Chen, …, Nguyen et al. Transformer Circuits Thread, 2023. ~1,240 citations. Anthropic interpretability work; she is a listed contributor.
3. **Discovering Language Model Behaviors with Model-Written Evaluations** — Perez, Ringer, Lukosiute, Chen, …, Nguyen et al. Findings of ACL 2023. ~800 citations. The model-written-evaluations methodology that became standard practice at frontier labs.
4. **Measuring Faithfulness in Chain-of-Thought Reasoning** — Lanham, Chen, Radhakrishnan, Steiner, …, Nguyen et al. arXiv 2307.13702 (2023). ~550 citations. Established that CoT faithfulness varies with model scale and that intervention-based tests are necessary.
5. **Towards Measuring the Representation of Subjective Global Opinions in Language Models** — Durmus, Liao, Schiefer, Askell, …, Nguyen et al. arXiv 2306.16388 (2023). ~415 citations. GlobalOpinionQA benchmark.
6. **Many-Shot Jailbreaking** — Anil, Durmus, Panickssery, Sharma, …, Nguyen et al. NeurIPS 2024. ~370 citations.
7. **Studying Large Language Model Generalization with Influence Functions** — Grosse, Bae, Anil, Elhage, …, Nguyen et al. arXiv 2308.03296 (2023). ~360 citations.
8. **Measuring Short-Form Factuality in Large Language Models (SimpleQA)** — Wei, Chung, Jiao, Papay, …, Nguyen et al. arXiv 2411.04368 (November 2024). ~340 citations. The benchmark she most often points to as a public artifact of her OpenAI factuality work.
9. **The Capacity for Moral Self-Correction in Large Language Models** — Ganguli, Askell, Schiefer, Liao, …, Nguyen et al. arXiv 2302.07459 (2023). ~260 citations.
10. **Evaluating and Mitigating Discrimination in Language Model Decisions** — Tamkin, Askell, Lovitt, Durmus, …, Nguyen et al. arXiv 2312.03689 (2023). ~150 citations.
11. **Specific versus General Principles for Constitutional AI** — Kundu, Bai, Kadavath, …, Nguyen et al. arXiv 2310.13798 (2023). The follow-up to the original Bai et al. Constitutional AI paper that probes which principles generalize.
12. **Question Decomposition Improves the Faithfulness of Model-Generated Reasoning** — Radhakrishnan, Nguyen, et al. arXiv 2307.11768 (2023). Sub-question decomposition as a faithfulness intervention.

## Public products and prototypes she ships under her name

- **ChatGPT Canvas** (OpenAI, October 2024). Collaborative writing-and-coding side panel; she describes Canvas as "the first full synthetic post-training" — the model was taught to use Canvas through synthetic-data-only post-training, not human labels.
- **ChatGPT Tasks** (OpenAI, January 2025). Scheduled/automated agent actions with the search and Canvas tools. Built in "under two months."
- **SimpleQA** (OpenAI, November 2024). Short-form factuality benchmark.
- **Streaming chain-of-thought for o1** (OpenAI, late 2024). Real-time reasoning visualization in the consumer surface.
- **Show Changes / Code Diffs for ChatGPT** (OpenAI, October 2024).
- **claude.ai chat interface** (Anthropic, 2023). The original Claude consumer product launch surface.
- **Claude 100K-context document upload** (Anthropic, May 2023).
- **Claude 3 Haiku** (Anthropic, March 2024). She led post-training for this fast / cheap tier.
- **Claude Instant 1.2** (Anthropic, 2023).
- **Inter Alia** (2022). Visual fashion search using GPT-3 — early hint of her co-design instinct.
- **Synevyr** (2022). GPT-3-integrated text editor — predecessor to the Canvas idea.
