# Aleksander Madry — Biography and Affiliations

Compiled 2026-05-27 from his MIT homepage, MIT CSAIL profile, MadryLab pages, Alan Turing Institute speaker profile, MIT Quest for Intelligence profile, and contemporary reporting.

## Identity

- Full name: Aleksander Mądry (Polish, often rendered Madry in English-language sources).
- X / Twitter handle: `@aleks_madry` (his MIT homepage explicitly links to `https://twitter.com/aleks_madry`, which 301-redirects to `https://x.com/aleks_madry`).

- Lab blog: `https://gradientscience.org/`.

## Current Affiliations (May 2026)

- **MIT — Cadence Design Systems Professor of Computing** (currently on leave). EECS Department. CSAIL Principal Investigator.
- **Director — MIT Center for Deployable Machine Learning (CDML).** Housed in CSAIL. Mission per CSAIL: "build ML systems that are safe, robust, and reliable enough to be confidently and responsibly deployed in the real world."
- **Faculty Co-Lead — MIT AI Policy Forum.**
- **OpenAI — Member of Technical Staff (AI reasoning research).** His MIT page states he "is on leave from MIT and currently based at OpenAI." Reassigned from Head of Preparedness in July 2024 (see file 03).

## Past Affiliations

- **OpenAI — Head of Preparedness (May 2023 – July 2024).** Founded and led the Preparedness team that produced the Preparedness Framework (December 2023).
- **EPFL** — faculty (post-PhD).
- **Microsoft Research New England** — researcher.
- **MIT — PhD, 2011.** Thesis received the ACM Doctoral Dissertation Award Honorable Mention and the George M. Sprowls Award.
