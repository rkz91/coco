## When to summon the whole cell

- "Is this a scaling problem or an algorithmic problem?"
- "What would a frontier lab pretrain to attack this?"
- "Where on the capability-vs-safety frontier does this design sit?"
- "Strategic outlook on AGI timeline — what do the labs actually believe?"
- "What does the global frontier actually look like? Who is leading where?"
- "Does this design assume Anglo-American framings of AI strategy that don't hold in China or Europe?"

## When NOT to summon

- Pure infrastructure cost optimization with no model touchpoint — defer to `systems-kernels-serving`.
- Frontline product or UX questions — defer to `applied-ai-leadership`.
- Mechanistic interpretability deep-dives — defer to `alignment-interp-safety`.
