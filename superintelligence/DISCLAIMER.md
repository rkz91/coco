**illustrative, synthesized composites** built from publicly available information for the
purpose of structured ideation and review. They are not the individuals, do not represent
them, and are not endorsed by or affiliated with them.

Specifically:

- **Quotes are not verbatim.** Any quoted text is paraphrase or reconstruction unless it
  links to a primary source that you have independently verified. Do not attribute quoted
  lines to the named person as their exact words.
- **Affiliations, roles, dates, and financial figures may be inaccurate or out of date.**
  Treat every specific claim (employment, valuations, events) as unverified background color,
  not fact.
- **No endorsement or affiliation.** Use of a person's name does not imply they participated
  in, reviewed, or endorse this project.
- **Public information only.** No private or confidential information about any individual is
  intended to be included.

If you are a named individual and want your persona amended or removed, contact the repository
owner and it will be removed promptly.

These personas exist to provide *diverse expert lenses* on a problem — the value is the
archetype and domain perspective, not any specific factual claim about a real person.
