Design Philosophy

The Inbox is your triage command center — not an email client. Items are organized by what action you need to take, not where they came from. CoCo pre-handles what it can (classify, dismiss noise, file FYI) and surfaces only what needs your judgment.

  • Sections by action type — not flat chronological list
  • Smart clustering — 7 identical GitGuardian alerts → 1 dismissable cluster
  • CoCo suggestions — "I think this belongs to Optimize" with one-click accept
  • Progress bar — gamifies triage, shows how much CoCo saved you
  • Stale badges — items older than 2 weeks flagged for bulk dismissal
